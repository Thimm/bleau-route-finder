Les Thunderbirds 6c+ Apremont

Tony Fouchereau
traverse fltr, wall, sitstart

Sitstart in the tunnel to the left of La Truite (sea blue n°9) with the two hands on an evident hold, low traverse in the overhang, take the slopers of L'Huître (sea blue n°8), downclimb at the angle, continue in the next face and exit at the angle of Foie de Morue or in the slab just after.

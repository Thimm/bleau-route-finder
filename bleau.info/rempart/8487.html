La Boule à Pierrot 7b Cuvier Rempart

Bertrand Lemaire, Pierre Gonzales
sitstart, overhang, slopers

Little overhang 15m to the left of La Pyramide Marbrée (black n°39) and Sideways Daze, in direction of Immodium. Sitstart and exit to the right via slopers.

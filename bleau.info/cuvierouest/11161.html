L'Anartionale 6b+ Cuvier Ouest (Biological reserve)

Max, Jean-Claude Valluet
overhang, crack

Avoid leaving the paths and keep your dog on a leash from April to July.

On the right of the big overhang of the "bloc des Artistes" boulder. Standing start with underclings (as in Oh Zone!), traverse to the right and exit in L'Anartionale (direct) with a vertical crack for the right hand (6a without the low start).

  • See also
  • L'Anartionale (direct) 6a
  • Access
From the parking lot, follow a path parallel to the road for 100m; the boulder is then on the right.

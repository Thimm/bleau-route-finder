Irrespirable 6c 6c+ Apremont Désert

Eric Allayaud
slightly overhanging, sitstart

Sit start to the right of Exposant (assis) with underclings, traverse to the left and exit with the arete near two green arrows. On the backside and on the left of the boulder with La Dalle.

Partage 8a Rocher Saint-Germain Centre

-
traverse fltr

On a bog boulder, 10m to the right of blue path n°4, after Danse de Printemps. Sitstart just before the left arete, low traverse to the right, pass the second arete, traverse low with pockets and exit on the slab after a third arete.

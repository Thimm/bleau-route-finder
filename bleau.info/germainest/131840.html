Le Casse-Tête 7a Rocher Saint-Germain Est

-
slightly overhanging, sitstart
  • Circuit
  •     red TD+ : 6 bis

Sitstart on the left with a vertical crimp left hand, climb the overhang and exit in Le Casse-Noisettes (red n°6).
NB: harder by staying left.
Update (July 2024) : the crimp left hand has broken off and the problem will probably be upgraded.

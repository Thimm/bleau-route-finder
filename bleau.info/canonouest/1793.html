Chute de Pierre (assis) 7a+ Rocher Canon Ouest

Grégoire Clouzeau
sitstart, overhang

To the left of Rêve de Pierre, on the boulder marked "F", along the blue path n°12. Sitstart completely left, climb the overhang and exit above by a mantle.
NB: rediscovered by Pierre Collantier.
NB2: it's also possible to start in the centre of the overhang.
Update (May 2017): the shaky hold right hand in the face has recently broken off but the problem is still possible with a dynamic move.

Les Yeux Verts 6b Franchard Raymond

-
traverse frtl, pockets, sitstart

100m to the right of Anthologie (red n°8), on the North-East face of a low boulder above La Route des Gorges de Franchard, in between the sector "Tectonique des Plaques" and the classical sector. Sitstart right, make the back traverse of Délire de Formes with jugs and exit to the left (red arrow).

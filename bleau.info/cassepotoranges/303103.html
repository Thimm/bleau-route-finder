Trav de Ouf 6c 6b+ Cassepot Roches Oranges

Frédéric Buc
traverse frtl, slightly overhanging

On the boulder underneath Nicotine, 20m below the character "L" on the blue path. Start in the right face with a jug right hand and the feet in the back of the overhang, traverse to the left and exit in Ouf.

Rocher du Potala    

Map
  • Author: Jean-Pierre Roudneff
  • Author: Jean-Pierre Roudneff
  • Author: Gars Des Bois

from the parking lot of La Vallée de la Mée (le Vaudoué), follow Le Chemin de la Mée for 400m and turn right at Le Chemin de l'Avenir de Vaudoué. The start of the orange circuit AD+ n°2 can be found 200m further. For the start of the blue circuit, traverse to the left at a small path leading to the first boulders of the sector.

Get on Up 7c Cassepot Roches Oranges

David Evrard
overhang, crimps, sitstart

15m underneath the blue path, 40m to the right and slightly above Nicotine. Low sitstart with a sloper right hand, climb the overhang with crimps, slopers and heel hooks and exit by a mantle with a pinch right hand or 1m on the left on other slopers.

  • See also

  • Topo
  • Cassepot Roches Oranges (Philippe Le Denmat) : 118
  • Access
Go up the path in the thalweg, turn left following the blue path on the plateau and go back down the slope just after it splits in two. The boulder is located 15m below, in an area where the trees have burned.

Rédemption 7a 6c+ Rocher Canon

Patrick Arias
prow, slopers, sitstart

On the backside of the boulder of L'Œil de Çiva, in an overhang with the graffiti "Jésus t'aime". Sitstart on the small boulder with a heel hook, climb the arete and exit to the right of the prow on slopers as for Mitoo.

Fatwoman Light 6c Gorges du Houx Oiseaux de Proie

-
overhang, crack, mantle

In an overhang 10m before Bicarburation. Start slightly to the right of Pif, Paf, Pouf as for Fatwoman, but exit directly in the right crack via a mantle.

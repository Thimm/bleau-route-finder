7-6-5 Franconia / 8-6 Bavaria (retour) 7a+ 7a Apremont Butte aux Dames

Finn Fischer
traverse fltr, crimps, sitstart

Back traverse of 8-6 Bavaria, in the slope above l'Allée des Vaches. Sitstart without crash-pad at the left angle, traverse in the overhang without using the top holds and exit by a mantle in the small face above the bowl.
NB: first known ascent by Finn Fischer.

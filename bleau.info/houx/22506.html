Billes de Glace 6c+ 7a Gorges du Houx

Manuel Marquès
traverse fltr, slopers, mantle

On the boulder with the character "B", along the blue path 5-8, 50m underneath Écaille de Lune. Standing start at the left angle in a small pit, traverse to the right on the ledge (without using the pedestal) and exit after the right angle on the face with the character "B".

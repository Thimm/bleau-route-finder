Mandela (assis) 7a+ 7b Apremont Vallon de la Solitude

Tony Fouchereau, Olivier Gx, Gérald Coste
wall, crimps, sitstart

On the boulder of La Moderne (strawberry n°20), on the right and top side of the main sector. Sitstart at the red arrow with an undercling left hand, climb the wall without the pedestal and exit as for Mandela with an undercling right hand.

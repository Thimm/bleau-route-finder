La Main Basse 6c Mont Aigu

Philippe Le Denmat
slab

On the same boulder as (and to the left of) Montée Divine. Standing start (without crash-pad) with a low left hand and a small crimp right hand and exit above without the holds of Le Dièdre se Rebiffe.

Astérix (droite) 7a Apremont Désert

Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, slopers, sitstart

On the first boulder of the sector, 20m underneath Dracarys. Sitstart left without crash-pad and climb the overhang as for Astérix, traverse to the right on slopers and exit via the arete of Obélix.

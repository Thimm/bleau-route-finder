Caronte Boom! 6c 7a Puiselet Mont Sarrasin

Luca Modoni
pillar, slopers, expo

On the East pillar of the boulder next to Les Trois Pitons, to the opposite of Intouchables Pensées and Chemin de la Postérité (black n°18). Standing start without the boulders on the ground, climb the pillar right hand and mantle up at the top.
NB: due to the height of the boulder (more than 8m) and the stones on the ground, this problem is very expo. Moreover, working out the moves toprope requires a certain experience of self-belaying...

Mojo Failing 7a 6c+ Petit Rempart

Lucas Chasles, Quentin Bry, Cédric Chatagnon
slightly overhanging, sitstart

20m below the big characteristic boulder with Le Majestueux. Sitstart with the vertical crack right hand and the low ledge left hand as for Mojo Rising and Petit Mojo (assis) and exit directly above.

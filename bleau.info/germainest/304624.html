La Vieille École 6c 6b+ Rocher Saint-Germain Est

Theo Konstantakopoulos
prow, slightly overhanging, sitstart

Along the blue path, in between the children's circuit and L'Arête des Comiques, 60m after a characteristic tunnel. Sitstart on the left of the roof with a crimp right hand and a slopy pinch left hand, take the crimp on the prow and finish directly above.
NB: a triangular fresco was sprayed just before the boulder in May 2019.

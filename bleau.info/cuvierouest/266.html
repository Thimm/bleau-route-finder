La Gaule (assis) 7c 7c+ Cuvier Ouest (Biological reserve)

Thierry Gounelle
prow, slopers, sitstart

Avoid leaving the paths and keep your dog on a leash from April to July.

Follow the GR1, turn left 50m beyond the boulder of L'Abattoir (white n°6 bis). Sitstart.

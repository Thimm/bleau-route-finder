Démoniaque (assis) 7c+ 7c Cuvier Ouest (Biological reserve)

Pierre Duroché
overhang, mantle, sitstart

Avoid leaving the paths and keep your dog on a leash from April to July.

Central problem on a characteristic overhang 15m underneath La Grotte Geneviève. Sitstart, climb the overhang without using the left arete and exit by a mantle on slopers to the right of the top of the boulder.

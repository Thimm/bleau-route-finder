Déforestation 8a 7c+ Cassepot Roches Grises

Cédric Chambellan, Antoine Vandeputte
prow, slightly overhanging, slopers

Evident prow on the boulder to the left of Cent Pofs et Sans Reproche. Standing start with a pinch left hand and exit on slopers.
NB: a sitstart is possible but it adds no significant difficulty.

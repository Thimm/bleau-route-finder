Chamarande    

Map

Take the way out Chamarande on the N20, cross through the vilage of Chamarande in direction of Lardy and take a paved road facing the castle. From the parking lot, follow the railway for 350 to 500m.

Access

🚊 🚲 The area is easily accessible by the RER C from the station of Chamarande (15 minutes walk). The train timetable from Paris Austerlitz can be consulted at the address Horaires de Trains.

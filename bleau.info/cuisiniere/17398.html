Don d'Orgasmes 7a 7a+ Cuisinière

Pepito, -
traverse fltr

Near the bottom of the slope, underneath Impertinence and Maudit Manège. Start left at a broken hold as for Le Réta Orgasmique, traverse to the right (harder with low holds) and exit above the last vertical holds as for Don d'Orgasmes (direct) or more to the right as for L'Orgasme Originel (7a+ opening method). It is also possible to continue on slopers and exit by the gully as for La Goulotte Orgasmique(7b).

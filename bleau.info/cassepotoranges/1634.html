Plein Ciel (assis) 7a Cassepot Roches Oranges

Régis Allayaud
overhang, underclings, sitstart

On the boulder just above the character "T". Sitstart to the left, and exit in the overhang of Plein Ciel using underclings and a mantle on the right.
Update (March 2025) : please avoid climbing on this boulder if bats are present in the underclings.

Mur-Der 7a+ Apremont Ouest

-
overhang, sitstart

Sitstart on the left of the boulder with La Relaxe (orange n°22), between the boulders with La Pince-Multiprises (red n°25) and Querelle de Palier (red n°29). Sitstart between the stones.

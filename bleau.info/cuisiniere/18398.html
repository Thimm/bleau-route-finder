Le Grip Mexicain 7c Cuisinière

Thierry Bienvenu
traverse, mantle

There and back on the boulder of Don d'Orgasmes, underneath Impertinence and Maudit Manège. Start as La Goulotte Orgasmique 3m to the right of Don d'Orgasmes, traverse to the left on slopers and then with the holds of the middle of the face to the start of that one, come back with low holds and exit by a mantle just above the starting point.

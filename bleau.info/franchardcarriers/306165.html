Whale Party 7a Franchard Sablons Carriers

Jason Kester
traverse fltr, slopers, sitstart

To the west of la Route du Louvard, opposite Sentry Post. Sitstart left, climb the arete and exit at the top as for Party Whale.
Update (September 2020) : the small hold just under the arete has partially broken off but the problem can still be climbed.

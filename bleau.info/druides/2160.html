Sakura 8a Franchard Druides

Jean-Pierre Bouvier
traverse frtl, crimps

On the right of La Route des Gorges de Franchard, just after the crossing with La Route Raymond. Start in black n°14, finish in black n°16.
NB: this traverse was opened in 1989.

Rocher des Demoiselles    

Map
  • Author: Didier Paupy
  • Author: Gars Des Bois
  • Author: Gars Des Bois
  • Author: Gars Des Bois

This slow-drying sector has two abandoned circuits, of level AD and D respectively. Many new problems have been opened, completing old openings, along the orange circuits.
Two accesses are possible, depending on the sector one wishes to visit. The easiest way is to park along the N152, 3km from Le Carrefour de l'Obélisque at Fontainebleau as for Rocher du Mauvais Passage, and to follow La Route de Valmy for 900m; the start of the orange circuit is on the right after the crossing with La Route du Champ de Manoeuvre. It is also possible to follow the N7 for 2km from Le Carrefour de l'Obélisque, turn right on the D63E and park the car 100m further (Parking Rocher des Demoiselles Est).

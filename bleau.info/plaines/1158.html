Le Solitaire 7a 6c+ Franchard Hautes Plaines

Eric Jarkoff †
slightly overhanging, dyno

Follow the TMF for 30m from Cœur Bondissant and take a small path for 50m above the circuits. Right angle of big overhanging wall (easier variant with a vertical crimp right hand).

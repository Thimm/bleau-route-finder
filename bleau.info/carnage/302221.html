La Vieille Verte 6b Cuisinière Carnage

Laurent Darlot, Christian Merimeche
slightly overhanging, arete, sitstart

On the backside of the boulder of Viol de Nuit. Sitstart in the back of the tunnel, traverse to the left and exit with the arete right hand (old green arrow at the top).

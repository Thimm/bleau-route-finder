Le Gymnécologue 7a Cuvier Sorcières

GJou, Alain Perros, Loïc de Saint Etienne
slightly overhanging, slopers, mantle

In a small chaos behind the classical area of Cuvier Est, underneath the North ridge. Standing start on the rightt and climb the arete on slopers.

  • See also
  • Le Gymnécologue (assis) 7a+
  • Topo
  • Mini Topo secteur Proueptologue : 1
  • Access
Traverse the classical sector of Cuvier Est by La Route de Bellevue and continue up to the GR. Follow the GR on the right for 100m up to a big bend to the right. The boulders are just before, 30m on the left up the hill.

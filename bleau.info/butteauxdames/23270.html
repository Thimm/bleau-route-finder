L'Étoile Noire 7a 7a+ Apremont Butte aux Dames

Thierry Vasseur, Sébastien Herbst
prow, slightly overhanging, slopers

Evident overhang 50m from Ailes du Plaisir, towards the circuits. Standing start with the arete right hand and a heel hook, climb the prow and exit on the right (exit in the left face is 7a+).

Ortograf (assis) 6c+ Apremont

-
wall, crimps, sitstart

At the bottom of the slope, on the West face of the boulder underneath Genghis (sea blue n°6). Sitstart, climb the wall on crimps and exit on slopers.

  • See also
  • Ortograf 5+
  • Access
With Le Pas de Deux (sea blue n°2) in your back, follow a small path that descends about 15m up to the boulder.

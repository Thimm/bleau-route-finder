Au Royaume des Elfes 7a Cuvier Nord

Grégoire Thibault
dyno, mantle, sitstart

30m to the right of Trompe l'Œil, in the middle of the slope, 50m to the opposite of Tortue Ninja. Sitstart without crash-pad with the ledge left hand and a big knob right hand, dyno to a jug from the sloper and mantle up above.

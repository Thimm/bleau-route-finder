Gradubidov 7a Cuvier Ouest (Biological reserve)

Marc LEHNER
overhang, slopers, sitstart

Avoid leaving the paths and keep your dog on a leash from April to July.

Right problem on the backside of the boulder of La Gaule, uphill 50m after L'Abattoir. Sitstart to the right with two underclings and exit directly on slopers.

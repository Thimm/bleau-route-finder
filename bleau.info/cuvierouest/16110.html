Tefal 7b 7a+ Cuvier Ouest (Biological reserve)

GJou
wall, slopers

Avoid leaving the paths and keep your dog on a leash from April to July.

2m to the left of Fluide Magnétique, just right and slightly below Neverland. Start from the small boulder on the ground with the lower slopers and exit directly.

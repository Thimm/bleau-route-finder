Narcose (gauche) 6b+ 6b Apremont Envers

Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, mantle, sitstart

50m to the left of Le Bivouac (red n°25), between two small valleys. Sitstart to the left of Apnéiste, climb the overhang and exit left with a mantle.

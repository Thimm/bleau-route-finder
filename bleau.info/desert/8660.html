Le Pilier du Désert 7c+ 7c Apremont Désert

Thibaut le Scour
prow, slopers, dyno

On the boulder with L'Ascension (yellow n°4). Jump start, climb the evident pillar and exit to the right or directly.

  • See also
  • Le Pilier du Désert (du bas) 8a+

  • Topo
  • Topo Apremont Désert : 39
  • Access
From the Bas-Cuvier parking lot (Apremont side), follow La Route de Clair-Bois southward, go straight on after Le Carrefour de Clair-Bois. When you reach La Route des Chouettes, go left in the forest.

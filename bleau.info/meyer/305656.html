Manche Longue 7a 7a+ Franchard Meyer

Gars Des Bois
traverse frtl, slopers, sitstart

On the boulder of Mèche Longue, just under the top of the hill. Sitstart with Dülfer moves at an evident crack, traverse to the left on slopers, pass the round angle and exit above the big oblique crack of the start of Mèche Longue.

S'il Vous Plaît 7a 6c+ Cuisinière

-
roof, mantle, sitstart

Small roof on the South face of the boulder of Argent Facile (white n°45), slightly down the slope from Pas Cool. Sitstart without crash-pad with the two hands on an evident hold, dyno to the lip of the overhang and exit by a mantle above.

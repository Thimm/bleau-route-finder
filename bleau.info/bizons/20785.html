Corne de Bisonneau 6c 6c+ Apremont Bizons

-
arete, slightly overhanging, sitstart

Sitstart in the middle of the traverse of Le Bleu Horizon (blue n°1) and exit on the left arete.

  • See also
  • Corne de Bisonneau (direct) 7a
  • Topo
  • Topo Apremont Bizons (Gilles Cottray) : 27-3a
  • Access
From the asphalted road, follow La Route Marie-Thérèse for 300m and go up the thalweg by a small path. The boulder is at the top of that one, on the right up the slope.

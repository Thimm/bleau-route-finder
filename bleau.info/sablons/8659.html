Le Journal du Hard 7a+ Franchard Sablons

Simon Brismo
sitstart, overhang, slopers

Just right of Sale Affaire, 10m to the left of Jokari. Sitstart on the stone, take a crimp and exit to the left via slopers.
Update (April 2011) : the crimp has partially broken off but the problem is still possible.

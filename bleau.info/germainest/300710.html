L'Arête des Comiques 7a+ 7a Rocher Saint-Germain Est

Thierry Vasseur, Tony Fouchereau
arete, slopers, sitstart

To the opposite of the blue path, on the left of the boulder marked "U", 60m from yellow n°22 by going up the slope on a small path. Sitstart without crash-pad on the right of the arete, climb that one and exit at the highest point.
NB: an easier exit more to the left is possible.

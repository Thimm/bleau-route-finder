Panzer 7a+ Franchard Isatis

-
sitstart, roof, mantle

On the small boulder 10m behind El Poussah. Sitstart in the pit, climb the overhang and exit by a mantle slightly on the left.
NB: more difficult since the hold at the exit of the roof partially broke off.

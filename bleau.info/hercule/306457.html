Idiotie (rallongé) 6c+ Roche d'Hercule

-
roof, mantle

Along the blue path, 250m above La Roche d'Hercule, on the boulder marked "I". Start in the middle of the roof at a step, low traverse to the left, climb the left arete and exit at the angle as for Rince-Mi.

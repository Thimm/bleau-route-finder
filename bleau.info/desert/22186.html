Lost in Translation 7a 6b+ Apremont Désert

Eric Allayaud
overhang, sitstart

On the boulder 20m to the East of Le Pilier du Désert. Sitstart at an obvious low jug with three green arrows as for Hypocondriaque, low traverse to the left in the overhang and exit by a mantle just to the right of Le Chaos (orange n°15).
NB: the original version is 7a+ but several more direct and more popular exits exist, all in between the original exit and the exit of Hypocondriaque, also in difficulty (6b+ to 6c).

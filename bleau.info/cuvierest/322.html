On a Volé le Frisbee 7b Cuvier Est

Laurent Avare
traverse fltr

In the sector of Le Carré d'As (black n°30), to the right of La Route de Bellevue, 50m after having crossed the blue path n°5. Start on the slab completely left, traverse with top holds, downclimb with a pinch, pass a second angle, take the underclings and exit past the arete on the right above a small boulder.
NB: the last face was lichenous in August 2010 but the shortened traverse exiting at the arete had been rebrushed.

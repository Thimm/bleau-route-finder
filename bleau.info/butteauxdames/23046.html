Bavaria 6c Apremont Butte aux Dames

Thierry Vasseur
slightly overhanging, sitstart

In the North face above l'Allée des Vaches, 30m underneath Ailes du Plaisir. Sitstart with slopers in the centre of the boulder and exit directly.

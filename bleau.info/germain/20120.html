Out Système 7a 7a+ Rocher Saint-Germain Ouest

Alexis Allayaud
overhang, sitstart, expo

Left version of the overhang to the opposite of Deus Ex Machina, 60m to the left of Les Yeux Plus Gros que le Ventre. Sitstart, climb the overhang diagonally to the left and exit at the top of the boulder.

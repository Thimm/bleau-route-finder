Transat 7b 7a+ Rocher Canon

Emmanuel Gand, Michel Pesrin
arete, roof, sitstart

On the backside of the boulder of L'Œil de Çiva, in an overhang with the graffiti "Jésus t'aime". Sitstart on the small boulder with a heel hook, climb the arete and exit to the left of the prow on slopers.

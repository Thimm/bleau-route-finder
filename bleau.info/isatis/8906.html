La Piste aux Étoiles 7a+ Franchard Isatis

Thierry Guéguen
wall, crimps, slopers

In the plot 765, facing L'Insoutenable Légèreté de l'Être, on the left of La Route de l'Isatis, 300m from the parking lot. Direct problem to the right of the wall; start with crimps and exit via vertical slopers.

  • Access
From the parking lot, follow La Route de l'Isatis for 300m up to a sparse group of boulders 50m on the left.

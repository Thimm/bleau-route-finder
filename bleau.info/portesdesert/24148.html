Cardiotoxine 7a 6c+ Apremont Portes du Désert

Tony Fouchereau
belly, sitstart

Left problem on the small boulder 15m to the South of Accrocheur. Sitstart with a good hold, traverse 1m to the left, come back to the right using a bidoigt and exit on the top on bosses.

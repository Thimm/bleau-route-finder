Le Toit Triangulaire 6a 5+ Franchard Isatis

-
roof, sitstart

On a boulder 5m to the left of the boulder with Le Bilboquet d'Isatis (blue n°50). Sitstart with both hands on an evident flake, climb the overhang to the left an exit left of the arete.

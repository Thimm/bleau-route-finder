Ludus (sans convention) 7b Cuvier Nord

Grégoire Thibault
slightly overhanging, mantle

On the boulder of Makhno. Low start just left of Dominus with an undercling left hand, climb directly the overhang and exit by a mantle with the holds of the right belly.
NB: this problem has been opened with the lowest crimp right hand and both feet in the back of the overhang.

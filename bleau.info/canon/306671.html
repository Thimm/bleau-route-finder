Le Bombé (du fond) 7a Rocher Canon

-
roof, sitstart

Sitstart on the right under Le Bombé (sky blue n°3), climb the overhang and exit on the right. Start with one hand at the back of the ledge of Vilaine Biquette and one hand in an undercling and climb the overhang without touching the boulder behind.
NB: first known ascent by Etienne Beauchataud.

Corne de Bizon (direct) 7a+ Apremont Bizons

Frédéric Buc
traverse frtl, mantle, sitstart

On the boulder of Le Bleu Horizon (blue n°1). Sitstart at the end of the traverse of Le Bleu Horizon (blue n°1), traverse on the ledge of slopers and exit to the right of the arete.

  • See also
  • Corne de Bizon 7a
  • Topo
  • Topo Apremont Bizons (Gilles Cottray) : 27-4b
  • Access
From the asphalted road, follow La Route Marie-Thérèse for 300m and go up the thalweg by a small path. The boulder is at the top of that one, on the right up the slope.

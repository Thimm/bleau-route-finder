Meilleure Mine 6c 6b+ Le Calvaire

Liam Thijs, Rob Denayer
roof, mantle

On the right of the boulder marked "C", in the Western part of the sector. Start low, without the pedestal. Climb the horizontal crack and exit left, in the middle of the roof.

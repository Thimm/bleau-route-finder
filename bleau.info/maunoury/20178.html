Kut de Crêpe 7a 6c+ Maunoury

-
pillar, sitstart

On the boulder of Le Vase de Sèvres (end of the red circuit), at the North of the sector. Sitstart to the left, climb the pillar without using the big gully and exit in Le Vase de Sèvres (red n°36).
NB: first known ascent by Stefan Warsink.

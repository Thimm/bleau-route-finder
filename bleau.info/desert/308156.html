Irrespirable (rallongé) 6c+ Apremont Désert

Hugo Meunier
traverse frtl, slightly overhanging, sitstart

Sit start to the right of Exposant (assis) with the big ledge, traverse to the left and exit with the arete near two green arrows. On the backside and on the left of the boulder with La Dalle.

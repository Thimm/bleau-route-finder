Abdolobotomy (assis) 7a Franchard Isatis

-
sitstart, pinches, slopers

5m to the left of De Vita Beata. Sitstart in the middle with a two hands on the low ledge and a heelhook on it and exit to the right via the summit with a pinch and slopers.

Tête Basse 7a 6c+ Apremont Brûlis

Manuel Marquès
traverse fltr, crack, arete

In a roof, 20m to the left of Cordouan, just under the South crest, 100m above La Route de la Gorge aux Néfliers. Sitstart left, low traverse with the horizontal crack and exit in the right wall with the arete left hand.

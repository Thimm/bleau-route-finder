Dévers et des Pas Durs (assis) 7c 7b+ Apremont Désert

-
overhang, pinches, sitstart

Sitstart of an overhang 20m behind L'Ancien (orange n°3 bis), to the opposite of La Dalle. Start with the very low sidepull right hand and a high pinch hold (or the vertical crack if you can't reach it) left hand and exit left (a more direct exit and the start with the vertical crack are a little harder).

Démoniaque 7a+ Cuvier Ouest (Biological reserve)

Tony Fouchereau, Guillaume Tournus
overhang, mantle

Avoid leaving the paths and keep your dog on a leash from April to July.

Central problem on a characteristic overhang 15m underneath La Grotte Geneviève. Standing startwithout the left arete, climb the overhang and exit by a mantle on slopers to the right of the top of the boulder.

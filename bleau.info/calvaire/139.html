Le Grand Bleu 7c+ 7c Le Calvaire

Jean-Pierre Bouvier
traverse fltr, crimps

Along the blue path, on the right of Le Petit Bleu (orange n°24), just after a sign on the rock. Start in the left face at a small dihedral, traverse without top holds and exit at the end.
NB: this traverse was initially opened in the rtl sense, but the reverse one revealed more logical. The back and there traverse has also been climbed (8a+).
Caution (May 2025) : a hold has become loose, be prudent with it.

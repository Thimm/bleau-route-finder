La Dalle d'Alain 7a 7a+ Apremont Est

Alain Thibault
slab, slopers, expo

To the opposite of Futur Antérieur, just left of Le Pilier (red n°27) on the boulder of La Salamandre (light blue n°24). Start in the cracked slab and exit left with slopers.

Faculté d'Orsay  

  • Author: Emile Brighi
  • Author: Emile Brighi
  • Author: Emile Brighi

This sector gathers boulders located around the campus of the University of Orsay.

Access

🚊 The access is very easy by suburban train (RER B, stations Orsay Ville or Le Guichet).
🚌 It is also possible to use the bus lines 6 and 7 (or 91-6 and 9).

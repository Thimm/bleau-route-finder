Plus Fort que le Crabe 7b+ 7b Cuisinière Crête Sud

Bertrand Lemaire
traverse fltr, overhang, pockets

Low sitstart on the left as for Beatle Juice (black n°5), climb the overhang diagonally to the right, continue on the right boulder and exit in Santa Barbara (black n°6) after a stone on the ground.
NB : an old number 7 is also drawn.

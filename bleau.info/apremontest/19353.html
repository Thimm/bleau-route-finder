Knees 7a 7a+ Apremont Est

Tony Fouchereau, Gérald Coste
slightly overhanging, underclings, sitstart

25m to the right and above Égoïste. Sitstart with the two hands in a big undercling as for Le Nostromo, traverse to the right and exit above with good holds.

Striés Guidés (gauche) 6c+ 7a Apremont Envers

-
roof, underclings, sitstart

In the "Grotte du Hibou", 80m on the left side of the Route de la Solitude (GR), to the East of La Dalle Dévers (red n°44). Sitstart as for Strigidé, traverse with a big undercling and exit above at the vertical crack.

Apremont Brûlis    

Map

This area is close to Barbizon. Park on Allée des Vaches as for Apremont Butte aux Peintres. Take Chemin de Bornage next to the walls of the houses and pass two green barriers. Soon after, turn left onto Route des Brûlis, this path leads uphill untill a left turn (paved road). The group of boulders is to the right (60-80m).

Access

🚌 Bus 21 from Avon or the railway station of Fontainebleau-Avon up to Barbizon Cimetière : 20mn. Then 15mn on foot / 5mn by bike via Le Chemin de Bornage (bicycles accepted in the bus).
🚌 Bus 20 from Fontainebleau Centre or Bus 23 from the railway station of Fontainebleau-Avon up to Barbizon Plante Rabot. Then 25mn on foot, or 7mn by bike via La Grande Rue and l’Allée des Vaches (bicycles accepted in the bus).
🚌 Bus 19 from Melun up to Barbizon Plante Rabot. Then 25mn on foot, or 7mn by bike via La Grande Rue and l’Allée des Vaches (bicycles accepted in the bus).

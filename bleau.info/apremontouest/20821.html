Jamming 7b Apremont Ouest

Eric Allayaud, Gérald Coste, François-Xavier Joubert
slightly overhanging, crack, expo

On the right face of Nez-Percés and La Balance (red n°27 bis). Sitstart on the stone, climb the overhang with two vertical cracks (without using the left side of the boulder) and exit at the top.
NB: this problem seems to have been climbed in the past by Éric Allayaud and rediscovered later by Gérald Coste and François-Xavier Joubert.

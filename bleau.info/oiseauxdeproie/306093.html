Le Bi Yo Back 7a+ Gorges du Houx Oiseaux de Proie

Yo Mrvn
traverse fltr, roof, bidoigts

On the boulder of Bicarburation, at the end of a quarry, under the plateau, to the left of de La Route des Gorges de Franchard, in direction of La Gorge du Houx. Sitstart left as for Bicarburation (retour), traverse in the roof and exit with a mantle as for Le Bi Haut.

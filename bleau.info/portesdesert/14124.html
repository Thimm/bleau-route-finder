Effet de Serre 7a Apremont Portes du Désert

Philippe Le Denmat
arete, high

On the 5m high boulder of La Fissure des Alpinistes, along the Route du Désert, in the Western part of the sector. Climb the arete right hand to the left of Le Pilier des Alpinistes (several methods are possible).

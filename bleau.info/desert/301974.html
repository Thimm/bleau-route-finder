Obélix 7a Apremont Désert

Irénée Bernard, Tony Fouchereau
arete, slopers, sitstart

Right problem on the first boulder of the sector, 20m underneath Dracarys. Sitstart without crash-pad (morpho), climb the round arete with compression moves and exit at the top.

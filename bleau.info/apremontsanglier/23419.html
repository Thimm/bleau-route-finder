Tics Tocs 7b 7a+ Apremont Sanglier

Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, slopers, sitstart

Eliminate on the backside of the boulder of orange n°18. Sitstart 1m to the right of the round arete of Souffle Court (droite) with an oblique hold left hand and a flat undercling right hand and exit without using the left angle nor the good pocket on the right.

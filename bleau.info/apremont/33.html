Les Caprices de Benje 7a Apremont

Y. Gourdon
traverse frtl, slightly overhanging, crack

On the boulder of Jiu-Jitsu, to the opposite of Le Mur des Lamentations (sky blue n°12). Start to the right of La Fenêtre (sea blue n°20) and exit in Modus Vivendi (14 moves).

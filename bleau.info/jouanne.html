Dame Jouanne    

Map
  • Author: Gars Des Bois
  • Author: Jean-Claude Valluet
  • Author: Luca Modoni

In the village of Larchant, take a road to the North for 2km (follow the arrows "Dame Jouanne"), turn right and park in front of the inn, at the parking lot Robert Paragot. The boulders can be found on the left side of the path which starts along Le Chalet Jobert (inn).

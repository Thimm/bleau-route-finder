Fenbren le Fou 7b 7a+ Roche d'Hercule

Loïc de Saint Etienne
slightly overhanging, sitstart

On the second part of the ridge after Rince-Mi, to the right of Pas de Panique. Sitstart to the right of El Velibo with both hands on the step and exit directly in the axe of the starting holds.
Caution (June 2018) : the starting hold has become loose!

Rince-Mi (rallongé) 7b Roche d'Hercule

Babis, Tristan damagnez
traverse frtl, roof, pockets

Start at the bottom right as for Trop Beau pour Toit, climb the roof to the left using the pockets, without using the lip of the roof and exit in Rince-Mi.

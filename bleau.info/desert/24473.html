Longévité (direct) 6c Apremont Désert

Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, slopers, sitstart

On a small boulder 10m to the North of Le Pas de Deux (yellow n°26). Sitstart right with horizontal holds and exit above with a good crimp.

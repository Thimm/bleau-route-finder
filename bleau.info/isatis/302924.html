Périphérique 7a 6c+ Franchard Isatis

Ben vdc, arne libert
overhang, slopers, mantle

At the end of the sector, on a boulder with jugs 20m above La Route des Gorges de Franchard, underneath Le Pilier Légendaire. Sitstart with big columns, climb the overhang to the right and mantle up on slopers.
NB: the problem has been opened without the jug on he lip of the overhang.

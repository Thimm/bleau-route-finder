La Ligne Noire 7b 7a+ Apremont Vallon de Sully

Manuel Marquès
crack

On the North face of a dolmen-like boulder with a characteristic crack, 30m above the blue path. Start as low as possible and climb the crack by jamming (the grading depending on the size of your claws).

  • See also
  • Cerbère du Désert 8a
  • Access
Follow the blue path upwards for 300 meters from from the Lantara oak (former parking lot); when arriving at the letter "N", you will see a boulder on top of two other boulders to your right.

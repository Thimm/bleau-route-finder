Penchant Fatal 7a 7a+ J.A. Martin

Bernard Théret
slightly overhanging, sitstart, expo

On the boulder of La Voie du Forçat (blue n°7 bis). Sitstart between the stones just left of the arete with the two hands in a good horizontal hold, climb the pillar, traverse to the right, dyno to the jug of La Voie du Forçat (blue n°7 bis) and exit in that one.

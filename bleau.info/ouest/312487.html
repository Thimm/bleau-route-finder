Curlew 6b 6b+ Éléphant Ouest

Max Opitz
arete, high

Arete in a group of partially brushed big boulders.
NB: we have no information about these boulders which might have already been climbed years ago.

  • Access
From La Dalle à Poly (black n°40 bis) follow Chemin de Blomont les Roches for 100m to the west. At the junction stay left and follow Chemin des Crottes au Fer for 400m. The problem is 15m to the left of the path.

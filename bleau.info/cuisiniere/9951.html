La Bouse à Dom 6c Cuisinière

Dominique Millau
underclings, mantle, sitstart

On the boulder to the right of Évolution (red n°9), on the right of La Fissure, on the small boulder to the opposite of La Fissure Fédoroff (red n°11). Sitstart, take an undercling and exit via a mantle, using crimps.

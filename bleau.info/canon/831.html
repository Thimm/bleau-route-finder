Il 7b Rocher Canon

Didier Gérardin
overhang, sitstart

Sitstart with the left hand under the roof, the right hand on the lip of the roof and exit in La Vieille (red n°21).
NB: easier than the original grade of 7b/7b+ since the left foot starting hold has become larger.

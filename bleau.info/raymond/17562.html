The Big G 7a 7a+ Franchard Raymond

Neil Hart
dyno

On the boulder with the character "G", on the blue path to the left of Fond de Taille (red n°6). Dyno from the big boss left of the crack direct and slightly rightwards to the hold left of the crack on the top (do not use the crack).

De Fil en Aiguille 6c+ Cuisinière Crête Sud

Philippe Le Denmat, Stéphan Denys
arete
  • Circuit
  •     black ED+ : 30

In the North face, in the slope above La Route Amédée, 40m before La Châtaigne. Start with the arete left hand, climb that one using the vertical crack of the face and exit at the top of the boulder.

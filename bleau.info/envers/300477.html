Clandestino (gauche) 7a Apremont Envers

-
slightly overhanging, sitstart

On the left of the boulder of El Paso. Sitstart in the roof with the big jug and exit direct between Clandestino and Jeu Sans Frontières.
NB: a big part of one of the starting holds broke off (august 2024).

Tourniqueton 6c Bas Cuvier

George Maris, Philippe Le Denmat, Martin Keller
wall, arete, slopers

Between La Fissure Morin (blue n°14) and La Daubé (former red n°9). Start just left of the stump, climb the wall using the arete of La Fissure Morin left hand and exit on slopers.

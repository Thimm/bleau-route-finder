Chute de Pierre 7a Rocher Canon Ouest

Grégoire Clouzeau
overhang, mantle

On the boulder marked "F", along the blue path n°12. Standing start to the left of Rêve de Pierre and mantle up above.
NB: rediscovered by Pierre Collantier.
Update (May 2017): the shaky hold right hand in the face has recently broken off but the problem is still possible with a dynamic move.

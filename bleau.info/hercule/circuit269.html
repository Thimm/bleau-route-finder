White EN Roche d'Hercule

Author: Hervé Béranger

This old circuit has been created by Gilles Cottray and Michel Coquard for children from 5 to 8 years old, with a few problems destinated to older ones. It starts just under the famour Roche d'Hercule. The circuit has been repainted in 2022 by H. Béranger of the CAF of Fontainebleau.

Accelerator 7b Rocher Gréau Pyrénées

Olivier Lebreton
arete, expo

8m high arete, generally climbed toprope.
Update (April 2022) : the last left hand crimp has broken off and only a small (and loose) holds remains. The problem is still possible with the same grade but has become more expo without rope...

  • Access
From a small parking lot 500m after the town hall, follow the GR up to a pont of view. The boulder is just underneath the path.

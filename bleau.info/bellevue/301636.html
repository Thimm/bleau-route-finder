Ulysse 7a Cuvier Bellevue

Marc LEHNER
overhang, slopers, sitstart

Sitstart with the big hold and exit direct without the arete, a little to the left with an undercling or more to the right with slopers.
NB: the first ledge of this problem moves a lot and threatens to break, so you should no longer use it as a hold and start with the ledge above.

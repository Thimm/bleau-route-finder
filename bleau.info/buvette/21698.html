Priapisme 7a 7a+ Apremont Buvette

GJou, Greg Gourlet
prow, slightly overhanging

On a boulder close to the parking lot. Standing start, climb the prow and exit by a mantle on the left.
NB: a harder right exit is also possible.

  • See also
  • Priapisme (assis) 7c
  • Priapisme (droite) 8a
  • Access
From the Bas Bréau parking lot, take La Route de Barbizon for 120m; the boulder is then 40m on the right.

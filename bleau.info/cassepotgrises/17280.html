Crampe in the Ghetto 6c Cassepot Roches Grises

Antoine Sippel, Henri Dokoninck
prow, mantle

Standing start of the prow 15m to the right of the boulder marked "D", to the left of the one with Double Axel.
NB: the back of the boulder being lichenous, it is recommended to downclimb the face to the right of the prow.

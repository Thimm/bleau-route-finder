Banlieue Nord (direct) 7a Bas Cuvier

-
wall, slopers

Exit wall of Banlieue Nord, on the other side of L'Orientale (red n°40). Start with a vertical hold, take two slopers and exit by a mantle on the bowl on the right.
NB: it is also possible to exit on the left (same grade).

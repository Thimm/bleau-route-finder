Le Bi Haut 7b Gorges du Houx Oiseaux de Proie

Christian Roumégoux
roof, bidoigts, mantle

On the boulder of Bicarburation, at the end of a quarry, under the plateau, to the left of de La Route des Gorges de Franchard, in direction of La Gorge du Houx. Start at the left pillar of the bivouac as for Le Bi se Marque, dyno to the left in the roof and exit via a mantle.

Excalibur 7a+ 7a Cuisinière Crête Sud

Philippe Le Denmat, Emmanuel Ratouis
pillar, mantle, slopers
  • Circuit
  •     black ED+ : 3

Climb the pillar directly, without the high holds on the right face and exit via a mantle on slopers on the round arete.
NB : a 6b+ right version has become possible since a hold in the right face has been brushed.

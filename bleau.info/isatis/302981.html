Périphérique (direct) 6a 6a+ Franchard Isatis

-
slightly overhanging, pockets, sitstart

At the end of the sector, on a boulder with jugs 20m above La Route des Gorges de Franchard, underneath Le Pilier Légendaire. Sitstart with evident columns and exit directly with a jug.

Double Entorse 7b+ 7b Cuisinière

Philippe Dumez
dyno

Double dyno without the arete to the left of Entorse, on the boulder of du Trampoline (red n°14 bis).
NB: a run & jump version leftward to the top in the middle of the boulder has been done by Thomas Willenberg, but this style of climbing is not recommended.

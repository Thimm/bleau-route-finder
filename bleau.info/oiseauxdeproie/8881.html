Fatwoman 7a 7a+ Gorges du Houx Oiseaux de Proie

Pepito
arete, slightly overhanging, sitstart

In an overhang 10m before Bicarburation. Start right, climb the right crack, traverse on the arete using slopers and exit as for Pif, Paf, Pouf.

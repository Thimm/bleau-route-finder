Métromotrice (assis) 7c 7b+ Franchard Hautes Plaines

Thierry Guéguen
arete, slightly overhanging, expo

Right arete of the boulder of Millenium and Métropolis. Crouching start right (sitstart for tall climbers), make an ascending traverse on the arete and exit to the left of Métropolis.

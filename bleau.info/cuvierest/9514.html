Le Brut 6c+ 7a Cuvier Est

Govert Schoof, Marc Beringen
slightly overhanging, underclings

A not very high boulder on the left side the blue path, 50m from Duroxmanie (black n°29) in the direction of Rempart, before L'Aérolithe. Start low using underclings, finish to the right.

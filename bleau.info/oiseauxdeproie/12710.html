Bloc l'Éponge 6c 6b+ Gorges du Houx Oiseaux de Proie

Christian Roumégoux
roof, bidoigts, mantle

On the boulder of Bicarburation, at the end of a quarry, under the plateau, to the left of de La Route des Gorges de Franchard, in direction of La Gorge du Houx. Start at the right pillar of the bivouac, climb the roof and exit via a mantle.

Jus de Pierre 7a Franchard Meyer

Jason Kester
wall, pillar

20m southwest of the crossing of Route Amédée and Route Carnage, clearly visible from that intersection. Start with the arete right hand.
NB: please do not scrub the moss from the right face, there are no holds under it that make either side of the arete easier to climb and it looks a lot nicer to have it in place.

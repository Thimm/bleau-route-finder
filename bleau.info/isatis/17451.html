Live on Mars 6b Franchard Isatis

Ivan Moreels
slab

Just under the traverse of You've Come a Long Way, Baby, 50m underneath L'Arrache Cœur.
NB: the difficulty depends on how far left you go to the top; the more to the left, the easier (see the picture with the red arrow and the video with Bram Van Geert to know the original version).

  • Topo
  • Isatis secteur Insoutenable (Ivan Moreels) : 29
  • Access
From the parking lot, follow La Route de l'Isatis and take a small path to the opposite of the sector of La Piste aux Étoiles, 50m before Screaming Tree. The boulder is at the middle of the slope towards L'Arrache Cœur.

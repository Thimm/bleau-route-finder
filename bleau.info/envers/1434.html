Paul's Boutique 7a+ Apremont Envers

-
slightly overhanging, slopers

Just right of L'Apparemment, to the left of La Douce-Amère (red n°37). Low start with both hands in the undercling and exit by convention with the little oblique crack and the sloper to the right.

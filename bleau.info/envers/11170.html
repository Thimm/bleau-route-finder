Clandestino 7b Apremont Envers

Max
roof, slopers, sitstart

On the left of the boulder of El Paso. Sitstart in the roof, traverse left to the big jug, climb a little to the right and exit with the slopers to the left of the belly of El Paso.
NB: like many other problems in this boulder, it's more of a game than an independent problem (see the line on the situation photo). The original problem starts to the right in the roof but a shorter variant became popular.
NB: a big part of one of the starting holds broke off (august 2024).

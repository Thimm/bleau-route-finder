La Belle Proue (assis) 7a Franchard Raymond

Jean-Pierre Canceill, Gilles C, Lionel Fraissard
prow, crimps, sitstart

Just right of Graine de Brute (red n°18), underneath Sacrifice. Sitstart without crash-pad and climb the evident prow with crimps.

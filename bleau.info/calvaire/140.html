L'Aplat du Gain 8a 7c+ Le Calvaire

Alain Ghersen
slightly overhanging, slopers, expo

2m to the left of L’Arquée du Gain. Start in the slightly overhanging wall and exit by a dynamic move (opening method) or static with a pinch/mono right hand (7c/7c+).

Hi Neil - Anonymous (01-02-2012 09:41)
You jam your right knee under the roof to reach a one finger pocket high up right, that you take as a pinch grip. Then put your left foot in a slot just below the left hand starting hold and reach up to grab l'aplat du gain (or the slanting crimper just below) with your left hand. Right hand up into the incut crimp and from there continue as mister Rouhling on the photo:-)

Toutes des Précieuses 7a+ Franchard Hauts Sablons

Manuel Marquès
traverse frtl, slopers, sitstart

In the overhang of "Les Précieuses", on the crest, 30m above the GR, between the main sector and the chaos with Brulaap. Sitstart at the right ball, low traverse on slopers and exit above the left ball.

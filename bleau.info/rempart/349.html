Hyper Tension 8a 7c+ Cuvier Rempart

Marc Le Menestrel
underclings, crimps, dyno

Direct version of Haute Tension, at the extreme right of Le Rempart, on the boulder to the right of Le Bide de la Deudon (black n°28) Update (January 2016): a new method using a heelhook to the right first reduced the grade to 7c/7c+, after that a part of the left foot hold broke... so the original 8a grade is perhaps the most realistic!

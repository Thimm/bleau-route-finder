Grosse Colère 7a Franchard Raymond

Pepito
traverse frtl, crack, sitstart

Caution (June 2024) : the big flake on the left of the start of Fond de Taille (red n°6) has become very loose and could collapse one day.
On the boulder with the character la lettrine "G". Sitstart to the right in Le Bel Interlude (red n°5), traverse to the left with horizontal holds and exit at the second crack.

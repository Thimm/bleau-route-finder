Moby Dick 7c 7b+ Cuvier Bellevue

Thierry Gounelle
overhang

20m above Gin te Nique, on the crest, to the left of a small path going up the slope in a thalweg above Le Bivouac. Sitstart in the overhang and exit slightly to the left.

Dévers et des Pas Durs 7a Apremont Désert

Patrick Arias, Jérôme Chaput
overhang, slopers

Overhang to the opposite of La Dalle. Start with a good sidepull left hand and a sidepull at the same height right hand and exit slightly left.
NB: starting with the right hand more to the right makes the problem a little easier.

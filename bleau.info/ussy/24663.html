Thank You, ça Tend 6c 6c+ Mont Ussy

Frédéric Buc, Gilles Cottray
overhang, sitstart
  • Circuit
  •     red ED : 26

Low sitstart under the prow with a good undercling, catch the start of La Bouche Rit (red n°28), take the big pocket on the left and exit directly.
NB: 4+ with a standing startt.

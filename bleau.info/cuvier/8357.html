Le Réta Authenac 6b Bas Cuvier

-
mantle

Mantle on a big ledge to the left of La Vire Authenac (red n°8). Start with a hold left hand, jump to the big hold and exit with a mantle.
NB: originally graded 5+ and 6b with a traverse, probably from the right. The exact information has been lost and most repeaters rate the mantle itself as 6b.

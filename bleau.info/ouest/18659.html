Grosminet 7b 7b+ Éléphant Ouest

Alain Dupas
roof, sitstart

To the right of Le Grand Toit. Sitstart to the left of the small boulder marked "E" (without that one) and exit directly in the dihedral before the exit as for Un Jeu pour Toit.
NB: first known ascent by Alain Dupas.

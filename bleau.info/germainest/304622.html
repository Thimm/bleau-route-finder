Le Glouton 7b 7a+ Rocher Saint-Germain Est

Theo Konstantakopoulos
prow, overhang, sitstart

Along the blue path, in between the children's circuit and L'Arête des Comiques, 60m after a characteristic tunnel. Sitstart without crash-pad in the roof with small crimps and climb the overhang above.
NB: a triangular fresco was sprayed just before the boulder in May 2019.

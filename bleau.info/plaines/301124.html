Trou Noir 7a Franchard Hautes Plaines

Tony Fouchereau
traverse, crack, sitstart

On the boulder of Le Surplomb (yellow n°25), 15m above Le Solitaire. Low sitstart on the left of the little pit as for Black Hole Sun, traverse to the right via the crack, and then to the left on the ledge and exit above the starting point.

Fenbren le Gauche 7a Roche d'Hercule

Marc LEHNER, Louis Dalençon
slightly overhanging, dyno, sitstart

On the second part of the ridge after Rince-Mi. Sitstart to the right of El Velibo with both hands on the step as for Fenbren le Fou but exit on the left by a dynamic move from the arete.
Caution (June 2018): the starting hold has become loose!

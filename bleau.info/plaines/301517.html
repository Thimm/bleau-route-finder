Le Surplomb de la Coquille (assis gauche) 7a Franchard Hautes Plaines

-
slightly overhanging

On an isolated boulder underneath Le Débroussaillage (yellow n°23), on the hill by following the path starting from Beurre Marga. Sitstart on the left of the pit and exit in Surplomb de la Coquille.

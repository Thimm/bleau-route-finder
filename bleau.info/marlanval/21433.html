Realist 8a+ 8a Marlanval (Hunting area)

Nalle Hukkataival
prow, slopers

No climbing during hunting season: from mid-September until March.

In a well hidden pit, about 100m behind and to the left of Elephunk. Low start with the arete right hand and left hand on sloper, climb the prow to the left on slopers and exit above.

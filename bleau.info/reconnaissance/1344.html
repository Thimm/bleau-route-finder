Œil pour Œil 7a La Reconnaissance

Eric Allayaud
slightly overhanging, bidoigts

On the left side of the boulder of Continuum. Start left with right hand on the pinch underneath the pocket, take the bidoigt without using the holds of Vengeance and exit directly.

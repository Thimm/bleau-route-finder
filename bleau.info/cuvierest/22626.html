Nile Delta (assis) 7a+ Cuvier Est

Balazs Bajusz
wall, mantle, sitstart

In the sector of La Trivellini (black n°20), on the left face of the boulder of La Digitale / La Fissure du Crocodile (black n°22). Sitstart just left of Echoes with a crimp right hand and a slopy sidepull for the left one, dyno right hand to the ledge, mantle up on it, climb the wall above and exit by another mantle.

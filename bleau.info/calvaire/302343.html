Illuminati 7c Le Calvaire

Manuel Marquès
slightly overhanging, slopers, high

Left problem in the slightly overhanging wall to the left of Bonne Mine. Standing start to the left of the fresco, climb the overhang and exit directly on the right of the small 'nose'.

Ridicule 7b 7a+ Cuvier Rempart

Sébastien Frigault
belly, slopers, sitstart

On a small bowl-shaped boulder, 5m to the right of La Boule à Pierrot and 10m to the left of the boulder with La Pyramide Marbrée (black n°39) and Sideways Daze. Sitstart with a big jug, dyno to slopers and exit by a mantle.

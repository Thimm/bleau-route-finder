Le Labyrinthe des Ombres 7a Rocher Saint-Germain Ouest

Alexis Allayaud, Eric Allayaud
slightly overhanging, sitstart, mantle

To the left of Lydie'lle, on a boulder 40m above La Route de Luxembourg. Sitstart in the pit with the two hand on a vertical pinch, climb the overhang and exit by a mantle above.

  • Topo
  • Rocher Saint-Germain Ouest : 4
  • Access
Take a small path 50m before La Route de Hauÿ (coming from Belle-Croix); the boulder is to the right of that one.

Orange AD Le Calvaire

Author: Didier Paupy

The orange circuit, created by Robert Christe during the years 1975-1980, has been completed during the period 1982-1986 by Jean-Claude Beauregard and Pierre Jourdain. In 2018, Hervé Béranger, Axel Denier and Jean Cabane have finally redrawn it.

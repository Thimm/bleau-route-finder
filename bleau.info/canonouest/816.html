Les Eaux de Mortelune 7a+ Rocher Canon Ouest

Grégoire Clouzeau
traverse fltr

On the boulder just before and facing Le Maître des Brumes, beyond the boulder marked "D". Sitstart in a roof, traverse with low holds and exit on the right side of the prow in La Fissure de Gandalf (9m long).
NB: slightly lichenous (June 2006).

Ouzo 8a 7c+ Cuvier Rempart

Jean-Pierre Bouvier
overhang, slopers, sitstart

On the right boulder in the open spot just beyond Fatman in direction of Khéops (face to the left of Baptême de l'Air). Sitstart in the overhang and exit via a slab.

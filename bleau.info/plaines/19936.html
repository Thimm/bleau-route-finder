Kamasuska 6c 7a Franchard Hautes Plaines

Marc LEHNER
roof, sitstart

On the backside of the boulder of Doc' Jul, 30m to the right of Yodakov, just under the plateau. Start under the roof and exit to the right.

  • See also
  • Kamasuskatov 6c
  • Access
From the parking lot, pass Deux Faux Plis en Plats Réels and continue in the same direction for 150m. The boulder is just under the plateau, at the end of a valley separating Hautes Plaines from Franchard Point de Vue.

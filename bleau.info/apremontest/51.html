Le Piano à Queue (assis) 7a+ 7a Apremont Est

Jo Montchaussé
slopers, mantle, sitstart

Variant of Le Piano à Queue (red n°4) with a right low start. By convention, the crack is not allowed for the hands but may be used for the feet.

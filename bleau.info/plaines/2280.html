Light Woman 7a Franchard Hautes Plaines

-
sitstart, overhang, slopers

To the right of Cœur Bondissant, on the boulder to the opposite of yellow n°13, on the other side of the path. Logical sitstart from a boulder and exit on the right with slopers.

Cuvier Rempart    

Map
  • Author: Jean-Claude Valluet

From the parking lot of Bas-Cuvier, follow La Route du Luxembourg for 400m and pass the sector of Cuvier Est. The first boulders are after La Route de Bellevue, on both sides of the blue path and on the North ridge. Three historic circuits have been drawn, two of which have kept their colors red and yellow (for "sentimental" reasons), even they do not correspond to the modern standards for AD- and D circuits.

Approche Pragmatique 7a+ 7b Franchard Ermitage

Manuel Marquès
traverse fltr

10m to the east of Le Mur des Hésitations / Yéti and 10m above Franchard Beginner. Start on the left of the wall with a characteristic pocket left hand, traverse to the right without the pedestal and exit with the arete/crack (7a+). or without (7a+/7b).

  • Approche Aléatoire 7a+
  • Access
From the parking lot, follow La Route de l’Ermitage for 200m up to the top of a descent. The boulder is then 30m to the left.

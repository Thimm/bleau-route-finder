Opale 7b Apremont Ouest

-
slightly overhanging

Just right of Pièce de Résistance, on the boulder with La Renfougne (orange n°40). Standing start in the pit with the arete right hand and a vertical hold left hand and exit above (same exit as for Pièce de Résistance (droite)).

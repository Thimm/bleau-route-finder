Les Ailes du Plaisir (retour) 7a 6c+ Apremont Butte aux Dames

Tony Fouchereau
traverse frtl, crimps, sitstart

On the top of the hill (altitude 107m), above the Route des Ventes Alexandre. Sitstart with the arete right hand and a good hold left hand, traverse to the left, pass the angle and the start of the classical version and exit at the first or the second blue arrow (same grade).

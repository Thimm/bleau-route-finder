L'Ancien Six (droite) 6b Franchard Sablons

-
crack, slopers, sitstart

2m50 high boulder, with a sit start and the holds to the right, 20m to the right of L'Accalmie (red n°14) and Modulor, to the right of the small path that goes up the slope towards Jokari.

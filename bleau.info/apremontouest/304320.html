Saotoubi (assis) 7b+ 7b Apremont Ouest

Thomas Collignon
arete, slightly overhanging, sitstart

Just right of Saotoubo. Sitstart without crash-pad at the bottom of the arete with a crimp right hand and a sloper left hand, catch the arete and exit in Saotoubi.

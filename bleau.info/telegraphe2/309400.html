Karim Abdoul Güllich 7b+ Rocher du Télégraphe

Charles Albert
slightly overhanging, dyno

On the big boulder 8m to the right of Coup d'Épaule, in the middle of the slope, underneath the first problems of the blue circuit. Start with crimps to the left of Le Plat du Chef and exit on the left.

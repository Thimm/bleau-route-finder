Knees (rallongé) 7b 7a+ Apremont Est

Tony Fouchereau, Gérald Coste, Jean-Hervé Baudot
traverse fltr, slightly overhanging, sitstart

25m to the right and above Égoïste. Sitstart with the two hands in a big undercling as for Knees, traverse to the right, downclimb the slopers of La Traversée Perdue and exit by the right pillar as for yellow n°34.

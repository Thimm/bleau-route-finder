Hiéroglyphes (gauche) / Attaque les Grattons en Chaussettes 6c Apremont Ouest

-
slab, arete

To the left of the Route du Dormoir, 30m underneath Os Courts (red n°23 bis). Variant of the testpiece Hiéroglyphes; morpho.
NB: this problem, former red n°19 at the creation the circuit TD+ n°10, was rediscovered later by Victor Burger, with a higher grade due to the erosion of the soil.

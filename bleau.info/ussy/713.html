Ussy Cat 7a+ Mont Ussy

Gilles Cottray
overhang, dyno, expo
  • Circuit
  •     red ED : 11 bis

Start with the crack of Ussy tôt Fait (red n°11), climb it without using the good holds on the left, catch directly the small hold right hand on the lip of the roof with a heel hook and dyno to the top from a good hold left hand.

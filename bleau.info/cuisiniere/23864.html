La Pierre Ponze 7a 7a+ Cuisinière

Jules Nicouleau
slightly overhanging, crimps, sitstart

On the left of Côté Cœur, just under the start of the red circuit. Sitstart on the right of Glop! (red n°2) with the two hands on an evident ledge, climb the overhang and exit with a crimp left hand and the arete right hand.

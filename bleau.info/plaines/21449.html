Tom et Géry 7b 7a+ Franchard Hautes Plaines

Julien Forgue
overhang, slopers, sitstart

In the roof just above Métropolis. Sitstart completely left, climb the overhang directly as for Ah, Plus Facile!, traverse to the right and exit to the right on slopers as for Lapin ou Canard.
NB: first known ascent by Julien Forgue.

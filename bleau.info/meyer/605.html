Ouragan du Désert 7b Franchard Meyer

Christophe Laumone
traverse fltr, slopers, crimps

On the boulder between red n°24 and red n°25. Sitstart at the black point, traverse through the roof with Plains Toit as for Typhon (black arrow) but exit completely exit right in the crimpsy wall of Tornade.
NB: be careful with the starting loose flake!

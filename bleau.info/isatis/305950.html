Composition des Forces (assis) 7a Franchard Isatis

-
slightly overhanging, mantle, sitstart

Sit start with a good hold left hand and a side pull right hand, without crash-pad for tall climbers, with for smaller climbers and exit in Composition des Forces (white n°4).

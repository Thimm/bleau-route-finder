Gecko Tokay 7b Apremont Butte aux Peintres

Elric Besnier, Teddy Deschamps, Tony Fouchereau
arete, slopers, slightly overhanging

In the corridor, on the boulder to the right of Approche du Loin. Sitstart without crash-pad with the two hands on the round arete, climb that one and exit slightly on its right.

Le Corps Edge du Bonsaï 6c+ 7a Cuvier Rempart

Vito Grat', Fred
traverse frtl, slab, crimps

On the South face of the boulder of Le Rempart (Face Ouest) (black n°36), to the opposite of L'Angle Allain. Start right, low traverse to the left and exit in Bonsaï (without using the left angle).

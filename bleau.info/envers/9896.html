Tous les Délices du Mariage 7a+ Apremont Envers

-
roof, sitstart

On the boulder of El Paso. Start with the big pocket, take the bidoigt right hand and exit to the left; two methods, the easiest being morpho.

From the N7 (parking facing Bas Cuvier), take the Route de la Solitude. The boulder is on the left side, just beyond carrefour Felix Herbert, where the GR forks.

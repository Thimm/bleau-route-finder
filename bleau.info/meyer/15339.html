L'Ombre de l'Ours Noir 7b+ 7c Franchard Meyer

Thierry Plaud
traverse frtl, roof

50m to the left of En Attendant Hugo, past Le Trois-Huit. Start at the angle completely right, traverse on slopers under the roof in the tunnel and exit via the prow.

Les Beaux Quartiers 8a 8a+ Rocher de Bouligny

Didier Gérardin, Julien Nadiras
slightly overhanging, sitstart
  • Circuit
  •     virtual ABO : 2

Sitstart, traverse to the right and exit with the arete as Les Taudis.
NB: first opened by Didier Gérardin with a 7c+ version, but a hold broke off and the grade is now 8a/8a+ (first repeated by Julien Nadiras in this form).

  • Topo
  • Rocher de Bouligny (Pascal Etienne) : 10b
  • Access
From the N7, follow the Route d'Occident for 150m; the boulder is somewhat higher on the right side.

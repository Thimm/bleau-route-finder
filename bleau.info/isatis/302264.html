La Rampe Finie 7a 6c+ Franchard Isatis

-
traverse frtl, slopers, mantle

Shortened version of La Rampe Infinie, on the backside of the boulder of La Memel. Start completely right, traverse on the ledge of slopers and mantle up at the crack of Là où Finit la Raie des Fesses.

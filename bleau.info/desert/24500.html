La Squille (assis) 7a+ 7a Apremont Désert

Tony Fouchereau
prow, slopers, sitstart

Sitstart (crouching start) with a sloper and exit right with a pocket and the arete left hand. On the boulder to the right of Le Petit Pan (orange n°1).
NB: this problem has been opened as a sitstart without crash-pad (7a+/7b for this version).

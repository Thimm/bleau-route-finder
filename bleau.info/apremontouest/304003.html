Piece of Mind 7c 7c+ Apremont Ouest

Tony Fouchereau
prow, slopers, sitstart

On the boulder to the opposite of Sucre d'Orge (red n°37). Sitstart without crash-pad in the middle of the overhang with a high pinch hold and a low crimp, climb the overhang with the left arete and exit directly on the prow. Morpho.

Camalot n°6 7a 7a+ Apremont Fond des Gorges

Loïc de Saint Etienne, GJou
crack, slightly overhanging, sitstart

On the big boulder of Taipan, in the second sector, 120m after the crossing with La Route de la Solitude. Sitstart in the pit and climb the overhanging crack to its top.

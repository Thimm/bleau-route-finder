Boudin Noir 7b+ Franchard Isatis

Manuel Marquès
roof, crimps, sitstart

To the left of La Tire-au-But (red n°60). Sitstart in a small pit underneath the roof and traverse on crimps as for Le Lot de Boudins, but exit in the main face on small crimps 1m50 to the right of Donnant-Donnant.

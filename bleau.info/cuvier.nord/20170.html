Nature 7b+ Cuvier Nord

Thomas Willenberg
arete, slightly overhanging

Light version of Art and Nature, to the right of La Dérivée (blue n°16). Crouching start in the pit, climb the arete on the right (without the pedestal) and exit on the right.

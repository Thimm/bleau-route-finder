Bleautiful 7a Cuvier Merveille

GJou
pillar, slopers

Pillar 50m to the right of La Route du Cuvier-Châtillon, in the direction of La Reconnnaissance. Standing start with the crack left hand and the arete right hand and climb the overhang without using the part to the left of the crack

  • See also
  • Bleautiful (assis) 7b
  • Access
The boulder is between la Merveille and la Reconaissance, 50m to the right of la Route du Cuvier-Châtillon, 50m below En Esprit.

Les Ailes du Plaisir (raccourci) 6c+ Apremont Butte aux Dames

Frédéric Buc
belly, sitstart

On the top of the hill (altitude 107m), above the Route des Ventes Alexandre. Sitstart with the arete right hand and a good hold left hand as for Les Ailes du Plaisir (retour) and exit directly.

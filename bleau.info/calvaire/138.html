L’Arquée du Gain 7b Le Calvaire

Raphael Cabane
slightly overhanging, crimps

Between L'Aplat du Gain and Le Jeté du Gain. Start 2m to the right of L'Aplat du Gain and exit slightly to the left.
NB: an easier escape to the right is also possible.

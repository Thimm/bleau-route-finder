Moby Dick (direct) 7c+ Cuvier Bellevue

Thierry Gounelle
overhang, slopers

20m above Gin te Nique, on the crest, to the left of a small path going up the slope in a thalweg above Le Bivouac. Sitstart in the overhang and exit directly (diagonal exit to the left is 7b+).

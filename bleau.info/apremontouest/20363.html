Lullaby (assis) 7c Apremont Ouest

Emmanuel Gand
slightly overhanging, sitstart

Low sitstart to the left of Physique Théorique (red n°13) with an undercling left hand and a small horizontal hold right hand and exit to the right of Lullaby (direct) without the aretes.
NB: often downgraded because of a much easier than 7c start without the first move (starting with the pocket left hand). A logical but harder start, directly underneath the underclings, is also possible.

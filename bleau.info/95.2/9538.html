Coince et Danse / Rheingold (assis) 7a 7a+ 95.2

Yano Salaün
sitstart, slightly overhanging

To the opposite of Le Kilo de Beurre (white n°1) and L'Ange Naïf. Sitstart at the big pocket, make a knee-bar and exit to the right in Rheingold.

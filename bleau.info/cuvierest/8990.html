Atmosphère 7a 6c+ Cuvier Est

Thierry Vasseur
arete

In the sector of La Trivellini (black n°20), on the left face of the boulder of La Digitale / La Fissure du Crocodile (black n°22). Start in the pit just right of Echoes with the two hands on the slopers, dyno to the arete and exit via that one.

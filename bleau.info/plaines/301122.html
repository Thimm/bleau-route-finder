Bossanovabrasil 7b Franchard Hautes Plaines

Tony Fouchereau
arete, sitstart

Left arete on a boulder exposed to the West, above Le Solitaire, along a small path. Sitstart without crash-pad, climb the arete, dyno to the right one and exit in Bossanova.

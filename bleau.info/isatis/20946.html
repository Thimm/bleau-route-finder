Le Bivouac de Pilou 6c 6c+ Franchard Isatis

Thierry Vasseur
slightly overhanging, mantle, sitstart

On the boulder in between Treenee and OK Corral, 30m to the right of La Mâchoire (red n°30). Sitstart on the stones on the ground and exit by the overhang above as for Le Réta de Pilou.

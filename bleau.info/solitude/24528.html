Deltaroc (assis) 7a Apremont Vallon de la Solitude

Jérémy Lecouvert, Bertrand Ville
arete, slopers, sitstart

Sitstart without crash-pad in the pit, climb the overhang, traverse above the boulder on the ground and exit in Deltaroc.
NB: take care of the starting hold which has become slightly loose.

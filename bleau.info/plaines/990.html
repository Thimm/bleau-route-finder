Le Surplomb de la Coquille (direct) 7a 7a+ Franchard Hautes Plaines

-
overhang, dyno

Direct exit of Le Surplomb de la Coquille without last vertical hold, on an isolated boulder underneath Le Débroussaillage (yellow n°23), on the hill by following the path starting from Beurre Marga.

L'Ouvreur Propose (droite) 6c Cuvier Ouest (Biological reserve)

Tony Fouchereau
arete, slopers, sitstart

Avoid leaving the paths and keep your dog on a leash from April to July.

In the North face, on the backside of the boulder of Écart de Conduite (the boulder to the opposite of blue "Philou" n°45 and Multipass). Sitstart at the right angle, climb the arete up to the slopers, traverse to the right and exit in the right face.

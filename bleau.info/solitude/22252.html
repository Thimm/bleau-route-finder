Madiba 7a 6c+ Apremont Vallon de la Solitude

-
slightly overhanging, belly, expo

On the boulder of La Moderne (strawberry n°20), on the right and top side of the main sector. Sitstart in the middle of the traverse of La Moderne (strawberry n°20), 2m to the right of Mandela, climb the overhang and exit with a high bidoigt right hand.

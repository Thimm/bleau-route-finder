La Maschette 8a 7c+ Rocher Canon

Antoine Eydoux, Pierro Desbois
overhang

On the boulder to the right of Les Grimpeurs se Cachent pour Médire (white n°22). Start with an undercling left hand in the roof and a crimp right hand on the ledge as for La Maschine, but exit to the left. Without the stone.

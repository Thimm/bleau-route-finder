Wolverine 7c 7b Apremont Désert

Fabien Marchand, Tony Fouchereau, Teddy Deschamps
roof, sitstart

On the boulder to the left of Évolutions (yellow n°14). Sitstart between stones in the middle of the roof with a good vertical hold and a small sloper and exit left of the crack.
NB: using a kneepad lowers the difficulty to about 7b.
NB: another version (7b+ without kneepad) starting 1m to the right with a crimp left hand is also possible.

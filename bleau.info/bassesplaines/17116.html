Longbow 7b Franchard Basses Plaines

Yano Salaün
overhang, sitstart

On the crest, 15m to the right of a characteristic "aerolithe". Sitstart in pit, in the middle of the 45° overhang and climb it with a dynamic move and a heel hook.

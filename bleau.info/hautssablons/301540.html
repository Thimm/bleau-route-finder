Les Précieuses (par le haut) 7a Franchard Hauts Sablons

Manuel Marquès
overhang, traverse, slopers

In the overhang of "Les Précieuses", on the crest, 30m above the GR, between the main sector and the chaos with Brulaap. Standing start in the back of the overhang, traverse in the gut with the oblique crack and body jammings and exit above the left bowls with slopers.

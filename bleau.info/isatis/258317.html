Le Surplomb Gauche 7a Franchard Isatis

-
overhang, mantle

Between La Power-Lolotte and La Rigolade (white n°10). Direct exit via slopey sidepulls without the crack to the left.
NB: This rarely repeated direct problem is often mixed up with La Power-Lolotte just to the right.

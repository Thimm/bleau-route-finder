Compresse Nez (droite) 7b Apremont Envers

Tony Fouchereau
prow, pinches, sitstart

Right problem on the boulder to the opposite of Le Dinosaure Agile (red n°12). Sitstart, climb the prow left hand and exit by a mantle on its right.

Lévitation (raccourci) 7a Rocher Canon

Bernard Forest
overhang, sitstart

Sitstart directly with the two hands in compression and exit with a shoulder move left hand and an undercling left hand.

A big part of the big undercling just before the exit with the crack is broken. The original problem is still possible but it's a little harder now (january 2018).

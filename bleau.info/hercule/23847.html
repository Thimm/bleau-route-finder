Fenbren le Droite 7a Roche d'Hercule

Filip Notebaert
slightly overhanging, sitstart

On the second part of the ridge after Rince-Mi. Sitstart to the right of El Velibo with both hands on the step as for Fenbren le Fou but exit on the right with a vertical crimp.
Caution (June 2018) : the starting hold and the one left hand at the end have become loose!

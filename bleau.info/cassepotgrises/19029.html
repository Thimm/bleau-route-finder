Crampe in the Ghetto (assis) 7a 7a+ Cassepot Roches Grises

Henri Dokoninck
prow, mantle, sitstart

15m to right of the boulder marked "D", to the left of the one with Double Axel. Sitstart under the prow (morpho) and climb that one directly.
NB: the back of the boulder being lichenous, it is recommended to downclimb the face to the right of the prow.

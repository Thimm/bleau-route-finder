La Couille du Diable 7a Éléphant Ouest

-
roof, sitstart

Central problem in the cave called La Grotte du Diable. Sitstart with a low spherical hold and exit on the right.
NB: please avoid this problem because of fragile engravings.

  • See also
  • Extraction des Enfers 6c+
  • Access
From L'Éléphant, follow the GR13 in the direction of L'Éléphant Ouest. Turn left onto SB19 just before the boulder with Transpotting. Walk straight on where the SB19 turns right (stairs). Keep following this path up to the ridge. The cave is just below the ridge.

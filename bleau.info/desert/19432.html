Le Teletebbies 7a Apremont Désert

Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, mantle, sitstart

On the boulder of Rubik's Cube, to the opposite of Le Pilier du Désert. Sitstart at the angle to the right of La Poignée de Métro (orange n°8 bis) with small holds, take slopers on the right and exit by a mantle.

Albarrableau 7b+ Apremont Vallon de la Solitude

Tony Fouchereau
roof, prow, pockets

In a big isolated overhang in the vegetation. Low sitstart with the arête and a round pocket, climb the overhang and exit on the "nose" of that one. The exit with the right jug is allowed (same grade but less elegant).

  • Access
From La Route des Gorges d'Apremont,take La Route de la Solitude for 125 m; the boulder is then 20m to the left of the path.

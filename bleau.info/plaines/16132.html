La Mort Suspendue 8a 7c+ Franchard Hautes Plaines

Kevin Lopata
slightly overhanging, dyno, expo

30° overhanging face just left of Le Solitaire. Start from the boulder on the left, take the crack, and then a pinch right hand, dyno to the top and exit via a mantle.

Vous en Reprendrez Deux Doigts? 7b+ Rocher Saint-Germain Est

Maël Bonzom
prow, slightly overhanging, sitstart

On a big characteristic prow to the west of L'Arête des Comiques and under the viewpoint, 100m to the North of the sector Retour aux Sources, past the circuits. Sitstart right on the stone with a good hold left hand and a pocket right hand, traverse to the left as for Vous Voulez un Whisky? but exit on the 'nose' of the prow.

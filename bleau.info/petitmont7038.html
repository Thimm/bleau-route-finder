Le Petit Mont  

  • Author: Philippe Le Denmat

  • Author: Philippe Le Denmat
  • Author: Philippe Le Denmat
  • Author: Philippe Le Denmat
  • Author: Philippe Le Denmat

This small familial area, located in the plot 219, near Bois-le-Roi, is dedicated to children. From Le Carrefour du Pavé de la Cave along La Route de Bourgogne at Bois-le-Roi, follow La Route Forestière du Pavé de la Cave for 400m and turn left at La Route des Ventes Bouchard up to Le Carrefour des Ventes Bouchard (500m). The blue path then leads to the area, 50m further. Three sectors, separated each other by 70m can be found : the group of La Cabane, the group of Les Beaux Chênes and the group of Le Petit Mont. About 60 problems have been brushed, most of them being adapted to children.

La Fissure Wehrlin 3 Cuvier Est

Jacques Wehrlin
crack, chimney

The world wide first bouldering problem ever to be recorded: this crack in between Le Carré d'As (black n°30) and Duroxmanie (black n°29) was climbed for the first time in 1908!

Danse de Printemps 7c 7b+ Rocher Saint-Germain Centre

Jean-Pierre Bouvier
traverse frtl, slightly overhanging

Along the blue path n°4, shortly after the crossing with La Route Haüy. Start 1m50 on the right of the corridor with La Proue de Printemps, traverse to the left and exit before the angle at a characteristic bracelet, as for Le Printemps sans Danser.

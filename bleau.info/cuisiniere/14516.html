Pause Café (direct) 7a 6c+ Cuisinière

Dimitris Roussos, Kostas Roussos
arete, slopers

Start at the angle of the traverse of Pause Café and climb directly the round arete just left of La Bombasse (white n°15).
NB: first known ascent by Kostas and Dimitris Roussos.

Makhno (sortie par Ludus) 7a Cuvier Nord

-
slightly overhanging, mantle, sitstart

Sitstart at the left of the boulder with a big flake and traverse on the right as for Makhno but mantle up quickly above the two crimps (exit of Ludus).

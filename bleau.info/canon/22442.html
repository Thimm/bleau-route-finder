Charles est Stone 7a Rocher Canon

Frédéric Buc, Gilles Cottray
slightly overhanging, arete, sitstart

Sitstart in La Cave de l'Oncle Tom (black n°26) with a big undercling right hand, traverse slightly to the left up to the start of Charleston and exit in that one.

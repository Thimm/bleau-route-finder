Bois Rond    

Map
  • Author: imschattenphoto
  • Author: imschattenphoto

From the parking lot along the D64, to the opposite of the road toward La Canche aux Merciers, take a small path for 150m and turn right at Le Chemin du Bois Rond; the start of the red circuit can be found on the right after 150m and those of the other circuits are 100m further.
These can also be reached from l'Auberge du Bois Rond, by following the TMF and turning left at Le Chemin du Bois Rond (same distance; recommended in case of affluence).

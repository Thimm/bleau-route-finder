Tortue Ninja 7a 7a+ Cuvier Nord

Thierry Vasseur, Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, mantle, sitstart

On the North face of the boulder of of La Murène, in the slope, underneath Le Dièdre Moussu (blue n°13). Sitstart on the pedestal, climb the overhang with the crimp left hand and mantle up above.

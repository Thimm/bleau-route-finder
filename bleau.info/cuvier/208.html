method - Anonymous (20-12-2004 22:36)
me too,let's try....

i think all the big holds on the right [one and a half mtr.are forbidden]

picon-biere is just too the right off boucherie [about 50 cm.from the right start hold from boucherie]
there you will find a edge for your left hand a litle lower to the right a pocket for your right hand[standing start]
left feet under your body /right feet high a bit to the right.
after this you go with your right hand to a sloper side grip,left feet higher under your body and cross over with the left hand to a pocket,then it's easy to finish.

the sitstart is with to undergrips,first your right hand to the pocket [standing start grip]right feet high and then left hand to the edge [startgrip standing start]
really nice....

i have send pictures to this site,maybe they will follow later.

have fun....

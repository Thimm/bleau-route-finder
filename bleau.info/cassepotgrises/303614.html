Crampe in the Ghetto (assis droite) 7a Cassepot Roches Grises

-
prow, sitstart

15m to right of the boulder marked "D", to the left of the one with Double Axel. Sitstart on the right and exit on the prow.
NB: the back of the boulder being lichenous, it is recommended to downclimb the face to the right of the prow.

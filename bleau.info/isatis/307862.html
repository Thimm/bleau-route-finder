Les Bas-Fonds de l'Entre-Toit 7a+ Franchard Isatis

Sylvain corrignan
roof, traverse fltr, prow

Sitstart left in the back of roof, traverse to the right with low holds up to the central crack, climb the overhang and exit in L'Entre-Toit (direct), without using the good holds on the left by convention.

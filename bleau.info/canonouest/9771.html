Biketor 7a Rocher Canon Ouest

Pierre Collantier
prow, slopers, sitstart

In the slope to the left of the blue path, 50m underneath Monsieur Plus. Sitstart as low as possible with a crimp right, take the second hold left hand, dyno on slopers and exit to the right.

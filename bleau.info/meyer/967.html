Plains Toit 7c Franchard Meyer

Jacky Godoffe
roof

On the boulder between red n°24 and red n°25, 20m to the left of Toutes Peines Confondues and No Respect. Start in the middle of the overhang (black arrow; beginning of the traverse of Ouragan du Désert), climb the prow and exit to the right as for Typhon.

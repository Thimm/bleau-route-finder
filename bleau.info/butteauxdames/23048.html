8-6 Bavaria 7a+ Apremont Butte aux Dames

Tony Fouchereau, Gilles Cottray
traverse frtl, crimps, sitstart

In the North face above l'Allée des Vaches, 30m underneath Ailes du Plaisir. Sitstart at the right angle, low traverse to the left without using the top holds and exit on the left angle as for Bavaria gauche.

Le Casse-Dalle (assis) 7a 6c+ Franchard Isatis

-
prow, sitstart

10m underneath L'Angle Ben's. Sitstart (without crash-pad) with a crimp right hand and a low vertical crack left hand and exit directly by the prow of Le Casse-Dalle (red n°37) (without using the vertical hold of Le Trompe l'Œil (red n°38)).

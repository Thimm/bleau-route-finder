Saïgon (assis) 7c+ Apremont Fond des Gorges

Grégoire Thibault
pillar, slopers, sitstart

Evident pillar to the right of the path, 25m past Maniaque. Sitstart, climb the pillar with compression without touching the boulder to the right of the crack. The first ascent was done with a sitstart on a crash-pad.

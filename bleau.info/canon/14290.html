Compressman 7a Rocher Canon

Cédric Chambellan
prow, slopers

On the small "floating" boulder to the left of Full Metal Jacket, above Le Cap GAP (sky blue n°1). Low start with two bidoigts and a heel hook and climb the prow with compression moves.

Le Pied à Coulisse 7a 6c+ Apremont Bizons

-
wall, slopers

Old arrow between Smooch (red n°18) and Mooch (red n°19). Standing start with slopers without using the good foot holds on the right part of the crack, take an undercling left hand, dyno to a second sloper and exit above.

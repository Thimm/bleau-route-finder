Rocher Canon    

Map
  • Author: Jean-Claude Valluet

From Paris, take the N7 and turn in direction of Melun at Le Carrefour de la Croix du Grand Veneur. Follow the D142 for 4km and turn at La Route du Lancer (large parking lot).

Access

🚊 🚲 The sector is easily accessible from the SNCF station of Bois-le-Roi by following the GR1 via La Route de la Perlure and La Route du Lancer (30 minutes walk). The train timetable can be consulted on the website Horaires de Trains.

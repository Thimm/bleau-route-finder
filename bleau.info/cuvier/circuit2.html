Orange AD+ Bas Cuvier

Author: Didier Paupy

Due to the popularity of the area and the proximity of the former bivouac near Barbizon, this easiest circuit of Bas Cuvier developed by Patrick Krier is very slippery. Therefore, the difficulty is higher than the average orange circuit (not to mention it was originally a yellow circuit). Not recommended for beginners, unless you want to scare them away from bouldering... The start of the circuit is on the "Place du Cuvier": follow the Sentier Denecourt (Sentier Bleu n°5) from the parking lot for about 50 meters, to follow subsequently the Route du Sanglier when the SB n°5 makes a sharp turn to the right. The "Place du Cuvier" is situated shortly after that junction.

Cannonball (droite) 7a+ 7a Franchard Isatis

-
dyno

40m from Furyax in direction of Hautes-Plaines, on the left side of the boulder. Dyno from the sidepull to the good hold just right of the top. Many methods possible: double dyno to the right or with both hands in compression.
NB: the top hold has broken off (November 2022).

La Fin d'Exit (assis) 7a Apremont Envers

Babis
slightly overhanging, mantle, sitstart

On the boulder of La Douce-Amère (red n°37), 5m to the right of Apparemment. Sitstart on the left just right of the tree with a vertical hold right hand and an horizontal one left hand, climb the overhang with a crimp right hand and exit by a mantle above.

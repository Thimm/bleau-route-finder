Los Pasos 6b+ Apremont Envers

-
sitstart, roof, crack

On the boulder of El Paso. Start as for El Paso Droite but traverse to the right and exit via a crack.

From the N7 (parking facing Bas Cuvier), take the Route de la Solitude. The boulder is on the left side, just beyond carrefour Felix Herbert, where the GR forks.

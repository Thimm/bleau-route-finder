En Forme 6c+ 7a Le Calvaire

Liam Thijs
traverse frtl

On the boulder marked "C", in the Western part of the sector. Hanging start with the horizontal crack, climb the roof without the pedestal and exit left, as Bonne Mine.
NB: a 7a+ game without the top holds is also possible.

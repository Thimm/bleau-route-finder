Ménage à Trois (assis) 7a Apremont Ouest

-
slightly overhanging, underclings, sitstart

Right problem on the boulder of Le Glaive (red n°26 bis). Sitstart to the left as for Ménage à Deux, climb the wall to the right until the arete right hand and the underclings afterwards and exit at the top on slopers.

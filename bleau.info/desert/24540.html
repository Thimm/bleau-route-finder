Longévité 7a Apremont Désert

Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, slopers, sitstart

On a small boulder 10m to the North of Le Pas de Deux (yellow n°26). Sitstart right with horizontal holds, climb the overhang to the left and finish with the left arete as for L'Harmonie. Without the crimp to the right.

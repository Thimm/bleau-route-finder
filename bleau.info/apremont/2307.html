Jette l'Éponge 8a 8a+ Apremont

Greg Sobczak
traverse frtl

On the boulder of Jiu Jitsu, to the opposite of Le Mur des Lamentations (sky blue n°12). Start to the right of La Fenêtre (sea blue n°20), traverse to the left as for Les Caprices de Benje, but exit 2m further in Légitime Adhérence by a low traverse without using the jug of Modus Vivendi (7c+/7c with the jug).

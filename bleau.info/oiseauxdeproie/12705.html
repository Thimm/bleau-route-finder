Bicarburation 8a Gorges du Houx Oiseaux de Proie

Christian Roumégoux
traverse frtl, roof, bidoigts

On a slow-drying boulder exposed to the North, at the end of a quarry, under the plateau, to the left of de La Route des Gorges de Franchard, in direction of La Gorge du Houx. Start to the right and traverse on slopers as for À l'Endroit, pass under the roof and exit on the other side of the boulder, without using the 'doorposts' and the walls.
NB: this is the original line in this roof, many variants have now been climbed and all kinds of new combinations are possible; we've decided not to publish all of these and stick to the most logical lines.

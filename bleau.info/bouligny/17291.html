6717A 7a 7a+ Rocher de Bouligny

Steven Demets, Ivan Moreels
slightly overhanging, crimps, sitstart

Direct problem between the crack of Élastiques and the arete of Imitation Granit, on the wall of Hypotension (boulder with a star, just after the fork with blue path n°9.9). Sitstart in the pit to the left of the right arete and exit without using the holds of those two problems.

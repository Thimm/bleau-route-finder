Dessous Fripons 7b+ 7c Apremont Buvette

Eric Allayaud
overhang, traverse frtl, expo

On the top of the hill, behind the buvette. Sitstart (or crouching start for the shortest) right in the pit, traverse for 2m50, climb the pillar and exit to the left of the arete.

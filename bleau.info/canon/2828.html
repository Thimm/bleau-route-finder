Le Réta de Zermatt Express 7a Rocher Canon

-
mantle, slopers

On the boulder of Le Cervin (sky blue n°40). Start in the right face as for L'Impossible (sky blue n°38), traverse for 2m on a ledge of slopers and mantle up above at an old red arrow.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30-12-2020

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of bleau.info. We use your Personal Information only for providing and improving bleau.info. By using bleau.info, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection And Use While using bleau.info, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to your name ("Personal Information"). We will not resell or lease any information provided by you, or otherwise provide it to third parties. You are always entitled to inspect your data on your personal page. In addition, you may request the correction or deletion of your personal data. However, removal is only allowed if the data is no longer relevant.

Log Data Like many site operators, we collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit bleau.info ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of bleau.info that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages and other statistics. We use third party services such as Google Analytics that collect, monitor and analyze this information.

Communications We will not use your Personal Information to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information, unless you sign up for our newsletter. In addition to your Personal Information you may choose to provide us with “Problem Information” such as repeats, ratings, grades, notes and suggestions. We will use this information to (automatically or manually) improve the problem information on bleau.info. If we find that the information entered does not contribute to the improvement of the information on bleau.info, we may decide not to process (parts of) the information provided or to contact you with a request to clarify your provided information. In order to communicate, it is important to provide us with a working email address. In the event that a user does not respond to our messages, or when we are confident that (false) information was provided or was directed against other people, we can decide to close the relevant account. If you find information on bleau.info that is directed against a person or if you suspect false information was provided or directed against you, please contact us with the relevant details.

Cookies Cookies are files with small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your computer's hard drive. Like many sites, we use "cookies" to collect information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of bleau.info.

Security The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes To This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy is effective as of 22-05-2018 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will be in effect immediately after being posted on this page. We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time and you should check this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the service after we post any modifications to the Privacy Policy on this page will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified Privacy Policy. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by placing a prominent notice on our website.

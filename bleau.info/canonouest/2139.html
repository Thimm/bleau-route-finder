Père Dodu 7b Rocher Canon Ouest

Pierre Collantier
wall, underclings

Direct and slightly eliminating problem, along the blue path, between the boulders marked "F" and "E". Start with the undercling left hand at the bottom of the crack, take a small hold right hand and exit directly.

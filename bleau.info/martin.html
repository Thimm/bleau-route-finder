J.A. Martin    

Map
  • Polar explorer J.A. Martin
  • Author: Hassan Bensliman

J.A. Martin was an alpinist and a polar explorer who died in 1949 at the age of 38 aboard a ship bound for Antarctica, see the Wikipedia page https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/J.A._Martin_(explorateur). In 1950, a part of Rocher Cailleau was already named "Rochers J.A. Martin" under the initiative of Tony Vincent, who opened many classical problems there. The sector now includes 7 circuits of various difficulty and many non marked problems.

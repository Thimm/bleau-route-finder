La Joker 7a Bas Cuvier

Robert Paragot †
wall
  • Circuit
  •     white ED+ : 13

Direct wall, without the foothold left of the undercling, usually done by crossing left hand and exit without the pocket on the right.
NB: climbed for the first time in 1953, originally graded 6f but it's in fact the first 7a in Fontainebleau.

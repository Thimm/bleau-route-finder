Rencontre Plafonnique 7b Cuvier Nord

Patrick Arias
roof, prow

To the left of Le Surplomb des Dames (blue n°26), on the hill 150m above La Prestat. Sitstart with the two hands in the undercling, dyno to the ledge of the overhang and exit static or via a dyno to the right.

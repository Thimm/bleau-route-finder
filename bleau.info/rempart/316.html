Salathé Wall 7a+ 7a Cuvier Rempart

Olivier Carrière
slightly overhanging, dyno, mantle

On the North ridge, between La Grande Arête (red n°15) and La Voie de la Fin (red n°16). Jump start, climb the overhang with big horizontal holds and exit by a mantle above.

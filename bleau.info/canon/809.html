Lune de Fiel 7a Rocher Canon

Marco Dunand
traverse frtl
  • Circuit
  •     white ED+ : 3

Start completely right, low traverse after the first angle and exit in Le Chou-Fleur (blue n°22) after the second angle.
NB: 6c+ with the high holds at the first angle.

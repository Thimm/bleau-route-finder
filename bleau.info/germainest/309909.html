6mi 6c+ Rocher Saint-Germain Est

Franck Bainvel
traverse frtl, roof

Standing start on obvious holds to the right of Homo Cavernicus (blue n°34), traverse to the left, pass under the roof, join the start of Apocalypse Now (red n°34) and exit in that problem.

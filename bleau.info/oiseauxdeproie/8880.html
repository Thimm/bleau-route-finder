Pif, Paf, Pouf 7a 7a+ Gorges du Houx Oiseaux de Proie

Sébastien Frigault
slightly overhanging, sitstart

Left problem in an overhang 10m before Bicarburation. Sitstart as for Fatwoman and exit at the same point, by climbing the overhang directly with a fragile flake.
Take care: the flake hold bends!

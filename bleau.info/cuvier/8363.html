Le Participe Présent 6c 6b+ Bas Cuvier

-
slightly overhanging, slopers

On the boulder to the right of l'Abattoir (white n°6 bis). Start at the horizontal crack and exit directly on the left of the gully or in the gully as for Le Participe Passé.

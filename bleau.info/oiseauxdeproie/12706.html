À l'Envers 7c+ 7c Gorges du Houx Oiseaux de Proie

Christian Roumégoux
traverse frtl, roof, bidoigts

On the boulder of Bicarburation, at the end of a quarry, under the plateau, to the left of de La Route des Gorges de Franchard, in direction of La Gorge du Houx. Sitstart at the right pillar of the bivouac as for Bloc l'Éponge and continue in Bicarburation.

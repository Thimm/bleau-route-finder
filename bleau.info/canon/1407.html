Les Câlins de Kim 7a Rocher Canon

Pepito
slightly overhanging, mantle
  • Circuit
  •     white ED+ : 5

Overhang to the left of L'Araignée (red n°38). Start with the arete left hand and exit with a mantle to the right of the arete.
NB: the start is easier because a foothold broke.

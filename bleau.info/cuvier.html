This very popular and accessible area is situated alongside the N7 (the road between Fontainebleau and Barbizon), 1 kilometer away from (the bivouac of) Barbizon. Park at the big parking lots of the Carrefour de l'Épine, available at both sides of the road. A pedestrian tunnel is available to get you to the other side of the road, if you are coming from Barbizon; you can't make a U-turn on the road here. The first boulders are already visible from the road.

Access

🚌 Bus 21 from Avon or the railway station of Fontainebleau-Avon up to Barbizon Cimetière : 20mn. Then 20-25mn on foot by La Route du Briarée or 7mn by bike (bicycles accepted in the bus).
🚲 From Fontainebleau, follow La Route Louis-Philippe, La Route du Gros Fouteau and La Route de la Reine (asphalted up this point). Then take La Route des Ligueurs up to La Belle Croix and finish on La Route de Luxembourg (uncomfortable pavement).10km and 40mn from the railway station (35mn on the way back) and 30mn from Fontainebleau Centre .

Apremont Fond des Gorges    

Map

This small sector, in between Le Vallon de la Solitude and Apremont Vallon de Sully, has only be developed during the years 2012-2013. It is composed of two groups of boulders distant of 50m one from the other, above La Route des Gorges d'Apremont, just after the crossing with La Route de la Solitude, 200m from Onde de Choc. New problems have been opened in 2016 further, on the right of La Route des Gorges d'Apremont.

Access

🚌 Bus 21 from Avon or the railway station of Fontainebleau-Avon up to Barbizon Cimetière : 20mn. Then 15mn on foot / 5mn by bike via Le Chemin de Bornage and La Route François Millet (bicycles accepted in the bus).
🚌 Bus 20 from Fontainebleau Centre or Bus 23 from the railway station of Fontainebleau-Avon up to Barbizon Plante Rabot. Then 25mn on foot, or 7mn by bike via La Grande Rue and l’Allée des Vaches (bicycles accepted in the bus).
🚌 Bus 19 from Melun up to Barbizon Plante Rabot. Then 25mn on foot, or 7mn by bike via La Grande Rue and l’Allée des Vaches (bicycles accepted in the bus).

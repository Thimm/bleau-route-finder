Le Trou de Balle 7a Long Boyau

Alex Bremmer, Matt Moreau, Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, wall, sitstart

On the second boulder to the right of La Route du Mont Fessas, 150m from Le Carrefour de L'Émerillon. Sitstart without crash-pad, climb the arete with a small pocket and exit on slopers on the left wall.

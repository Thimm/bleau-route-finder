Le Surplomb de la Coquille / Conquérance 6c 6c+ Franchard Hautes Plaines

Gilles Cottray
slightly overhanging, crimps

Underneath Le Débroussaillage (yellow n°23), on the hill by following the path starting from Beurre Marga (white n°16). Standing start to the left of the pit and exit slightly right of the top of the boulder.

Renversement Dialectique 7a Franchard Isatis

-
wall, slopers, sitstart

Just left of Footrix, le Retour. Sitstart in the pit, take the big jug of Tequila Sunrise, but exit via the round arete and slopers.
NB: opened in 2004.

Les Cavaliers de l'Apocalypse 7a Cuvier Ouest (Biological reserve)

Tony Fouchereau
arete, mantle, sitstart

Avoid leaving the paths and keep your dog on a leash from April to July.

Left problem on a characteristic overhang 15m underneath La Grotte Geneviève. Sitstart, climb the left arete using the hold of the right face and exit by a mantle at its top.

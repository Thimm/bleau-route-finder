Sans Famille 6c+ Bas Cuvier

-
traverse fltr

On a boulder behind Le Carnage (white n°6 ter) and Cortomaltèse. Start on the boulder to the left of the crack, traverse to the crack using slopers, traverse on the second boulder and exit in the small slab on the right (18 moves). There and back is 7a+ (34 moves).

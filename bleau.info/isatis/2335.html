Nezzundorma 7a Franchard Isatis

-
overhang, slopers, mantle

To the right of La Montagne (blue n°12), on the boulder underneath Le Renard Bleu, 20m before L'Angle Ben's. Sitstart in a small overhang and exit via a mantle in a round prow.
NB: a direct variant is also possible, going directly up the crack, without using holds further to the left.

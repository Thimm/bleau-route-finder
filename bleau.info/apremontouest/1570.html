Haka 7b 7a+ Apremont Ouest

Eric Allayaud
overhang, dyno, sitstart

On a boulder in between Querelle de Palier (red n°29) and Sitting Bull. Low start to the left in a cave (green point) with the arete left hand and an undercling right hand, make a heel hook left foot and exit in Haka raccourci via a dyno.

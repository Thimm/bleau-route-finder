Cannonball 7b Franchard Isatis

Jan De Smit, Steven Demets, Ivan Moreels
dyno

40m from Furyax in direction of Hautes-Plaines, on the left side of the boulder. Direct (eliminant) dyno from the sidepull to the top, without the holds to the left and the right.
NB: the top hold has broken off (November 2022).

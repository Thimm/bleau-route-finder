Platinium 7a Bas Cuvier

Philippe Le Denmat
slab, crimps

To the right of La Pogne (black n°2) and to the left of Un Amour de Soi / Deux Doigts Coupés Fin.
Update (March 2023) : the right crimp has recently broken off but the problem is still possible without change of grade.

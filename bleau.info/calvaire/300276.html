Quelle Mine 7a 6c+ Le Calvaire

Filip Notebaert
roof, traverse fltr, mantle

On the right of the boulder marked "C", in the Western part of the sector. Start left as for Bonne Mine but traverse on the ledge and exit in Meilleure Mine.
NB: 6c+ with the pedestal for the feet at the start as on the video of Filip Notebaert.
NB: one of the handles in the roof is not completely stable.

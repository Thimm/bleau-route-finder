Prose Profane 7a Rocher Saint-Germain Ouest

Eric Allayaud
wall, underclings, crimps

1m on the left of Xixi's Wall, on the second boulder to the left of Les Yeux Plus Gros que le Ventre. Standing start, climb the wall with small underclings and crimps without the left arete by convention and exit above on slopers.

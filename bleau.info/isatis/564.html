Guêpe Ride 7a+ 7a Franchard Isatis

Olivier Penel
slightly overhanging, crimps

To the left of La Dent Dure (blue n°6), underneath El Poussah. Low start with crimps, make an ascending traverse to the right and exit almost at the same point as for Les Deux Font la Paire.

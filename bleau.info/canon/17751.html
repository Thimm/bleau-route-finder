Passage Canonique 6b+ Rocher Canon

-
slightly overhanging, slopers, sitstart

On the boulder to the opposite of Échec et Mat (red n°37). Sitstart, climb the slightly overhanging wall and exit to the right on slopers in La Clé de Ut (former red n°25).

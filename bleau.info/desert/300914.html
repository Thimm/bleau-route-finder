Le Teletebbies (direct) 6b Apremont Désert

-
arete, crack, sitstart

On the boulder of Rubik's Cube, to the opposite of Le Pilier du Désert. Sitstart at the angle to the right of La Poignée de Métro (orange n°8 bis) with small holds, climb the crack directly and exit in Tubbies on Telly (orange n°8 ter).

Électrochoc 7a Apremont Fond des Gorges

Stéphane Carcone, Xavier Gouraud, Tony Fouchereau
traverse fltr, slightly overhanging, slopers

Sitstart 4m on the left of Féérite with good holds, traverse to the right with top holds and exit on slopers on the right of the exit crack of Strate Eau Sphère.

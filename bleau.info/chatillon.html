Rocher de Châtillon (Private property)    

Map

Please behave respectfully; entrance and/or climbing may not be tolerated.

This sector is located on private property where climbing is no longer allowed. Please respect this interdiction!
This small and quiet area has been very active in 2012-2013, thanks to P. Leduby and JJ.J. Naëls who updated the old yellow PD and blue D circuits and F. Buc and G. Cottray who have complemented the yellow and blue circuits by an orange AD, a red TD and a black ED circuit.
Access: from Milly, take the D105 in direction of Boutigny and at the signpost "Le Pressoir", turn to the left in a small road and park the car. Follow the GR up to a crossroad of 5 paths. Take the first one on the left and turn left again after a fork. It is also possible to come from above by parking the car along the D105 at the last bend to the right before the descent (old road).

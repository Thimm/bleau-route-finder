Lost in Translation (rallongé) 7b 7a+ Apremont Désert

Tony Fouchereau, Benoît Faure
traverse frtl, overhang, sitstart

On the boulder 20m to the East of Le Pilier du Désert. Sitstart with a good undercling and a pinch hold, low traverse to the left in the tunnel and exit by a mantle to the right of Le Chaos (orange n°15).
NB: A more direct exit is easier, more logical and more popular.

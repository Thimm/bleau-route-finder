Le Miroir des Vanités Fissuré 8a 8a+ Cuvier Rempart

Jean-Pierre Bouvier
traverse fltr, crimps, sitstart

On the boulder with Haute Tension. Sitstart at the angle of Nulle Part Ailleurs, traverse to the right, continue with low holds in the second face and exit by the crack of La Fissure sous Tension.

Narcose 7a 6c+ Apremont Envers

Eric Allayaud
slightly overhanging, slab, sitstart

50m to the left of Le Bivouac (red n°25), between two small valleys. Sitstart to the left of Apnéiste, climb the overhang and exit direct on the slab (with or without the left sloper).

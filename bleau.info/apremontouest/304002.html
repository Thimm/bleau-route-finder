Piece of Mind (droite) 7a Apremont Ouest

Thierry Vasseur, Tony Fouchereau
prow, slopers, sitstart

On the boulder to the opposite of Sucre d'Orge (red n°37). Sitstart right with good holds, climb the overhang with compression moves and exit on the "nose".
NB: it is possible to exit directly on the right of the prow or (more difficult) on the left of this one.

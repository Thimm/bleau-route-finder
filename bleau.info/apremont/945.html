Faux Contact 7a+ 7b Apremont

Jacky Godoffe
slightly overhanging, jump

Beyond Le Toit Tranquille, slightly up the slope towards the North-East. Jumpstart without using the stone on the left. The start with the undercling to the right is 7c+.

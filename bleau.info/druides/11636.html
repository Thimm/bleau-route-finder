52 Gérard 7c Franchard Druides

Vincent Pochon, Julien Nadiras
roof, dyno

Right problem in the roof of L'Antre des Druides, named after the graffiti drawn at the bottom of the boulder.

  • Access
From the former parking lot of La Cuisinière, follow La Route Amédée and 400m after the classical Pascal Meyer area, take the blue path on the right bleu path and follow the signs indicating "Antre des Druides".

la Cle de Droite - Anonymous (30-01-2003 16:51)
The problem la Cle de Droite is the right hand variant of la Cle. La Cle is the original 6c+ problem of the white circuit wich finishes slightly left on bigger holds than the right hand finish on slopy holds wich is called la Cle de Droite (droite is right-hand in French). In my opinion la Cle de Droite is 7a and not 6c+ as stated in earlier guidebooks and this has nothing to do with height or holds used.

Le Mur des Lamentations 7b+ Franchard Isatis

Lucien Guillou †
slightly overhanging, crimps, expo

On the backside of the boulder with Douche Froide (white n°12) and Sortie de Secours (white n°13).
NB: first climbed in 1982.
NB: the last common hold at the top of the wall of Le Mur des Lamentations, Gymnopédie and L'Éternel Retour au Sol has partially broken in the middle (February 2021), as well as the moon-shaped hold before the traverse to the left. The problem has thus become slightly harder but the 7b+ grade may not be changed.

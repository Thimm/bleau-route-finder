Tyrion (sans convention) 6b+ Apremont Envers

Teddy Deschamps, Tony Fouchereau
slightly overhanging, sitstart

About 25m to the left of Le Masque (red n°49 bis), 25m underneath and to the opposite of the exit of Strigidé. Sitstart with vertical holds as for Tyrion, climb the overhang and exit with slopers and the holds in the (non brushed) gully.

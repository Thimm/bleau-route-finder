L'Année des Femmes / Far West 6c Rocher Canon Ouest

Philippe Le Denmat
wall

To the left La Dalle Perdue), along the blue path, just after the crossing with La Route de la Femme.

  • Access
From the parking lot, take La Route du Lancer until its most western point; from there, follow La Route de la Femme to the South over 100m and go further West by the blue path. The boulder is to the left of a pinetree, near the one with the character "C".

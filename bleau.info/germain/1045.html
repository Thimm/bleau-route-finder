Lydie'lle 7b 7b+ Rocher Saint-Germain Ouest

Régis Allayaud
overhang, mantle, sitstart

To the right of Le Labyrinthe des Ombres, on a boulder 40m above La Route de Luxembourg. Sitstart to the right of the pit, climb the overhang with vertical holds and exit by a mantle above.

  • Topo
  • Rocher Saint-Germain Ouest : 5
  • Access
Take a small path 50m before La Route de Hauÿ (coming from Belle-Croix); the boulder is to the right of that one.

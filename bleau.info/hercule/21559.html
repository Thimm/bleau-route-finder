Sotos 7b Roche d'Hercule

Olivier Roman
overhang

1m to the right of Rêve Errance, 100m to the East of Rince-Mi. Start in the back of the overhanging prow, to the left of the boulder marked "G", along the northridge blue path above "La Roche d'Hercule". Standing start with the lip of the roof and exit above with a characteristic small crack.

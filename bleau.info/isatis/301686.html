L'Entre-Toit (direct) 7a Franchard Isatis

Victor Fleutre
prow, slightly overhanging, sitstart

Direct variant of L'Entre-Toit (assis), on the backside of the boulder with El Poussah. Sitstart and climb the roof without the big holds on the left.

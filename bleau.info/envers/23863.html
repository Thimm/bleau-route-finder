Apnéiste 7b 7a+ Apremont Envers

Eric Allayaud
prow, sitstart

50m to the left of Le Bivouac (red n°25), between two small valleys. Sitstart to the right of Narcose, climb the prow and exit at the top.
NB: a small piece of rock fell from the boulder, which enlarged the undercling on the right.

Un Ch'timi 6a Apremont Envers

Jean-Hervé Baudot, Tony Fouchereau
crack, slightly overhanging, sitstart

Small orange roof at the end of the traverse of La Rallonge (red n°6). Low sitstart with a good hold left hand and an undercling left hand, climb the roof and exit directly.
Update (August 2019) : this boulder has become very fragile and it is recommended not to try it.

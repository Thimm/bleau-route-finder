\"Iceberg raccourci\" should probably be described as : standing start of the exit of Iceberg . Then \"Iceberg raccourci assis\" easily comes with : sitstart of the exit of Iceberg (meaning sitstart low on the right).
The confusion may come from the unusual way this group of problems have been named. Most of the time, the main name is given by the standing start direct boulder. Then variations of all kind may be opened (sitstart, extensions, etc.) ; those versions are usually named adding a definite extension to the original name. It was more or less the opposite with \\\"Iceberg\\\".

L'Art de la Diagonale (en traversée) 7c+ Cuvier Nord

Tony Fouchereau
traverse fltr, slopers

Traverse on two faces as for L'Esprit du Geste, downclimb before Le Théorème (blue n°8) and exit completely right as for L'Art de l'Exploration without using the top holds by convention.

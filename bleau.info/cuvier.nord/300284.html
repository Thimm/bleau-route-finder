Makhno 7a Cuvier Nord

Max, Alain Denis
traverse fltr, sitstart

In the slope, just left of a path towards Rencontre Plafonnique. Sitstart at the left of the boulder with a big flake, traverse on the right with two crimps and exit with evident jugs.
